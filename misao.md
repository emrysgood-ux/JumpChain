## The Overprotective Brother

Misao's defining characteristic is his obsessive protectiveness of Mihoshi. This manifests as:
- Constant worry about her safety
- Resentment of anyone who "endangers" her
- Willingness to abuse his position to "protect" her
- Inability to see Mihoshi as a capable adult

### The Sister Complex
Misao's feelings for Mihoshi border on obsessive:
- Views her as fragile (she isn't)
- Sees threats everywhere (mostly imaginary)
- Cannot accept that she's happy without him
- His "protection" often causes more problems than it solves

*üìù Misao's love for his sister is genuine but pathologically expressed.*

## The Kuramitsu Family

The Kuramitsu family is one of the most powerful clans in the galaxy:
- Control significant GP influence through Grandfather Minami
- Seniwan species grants long lifespans
- Connected to Washu through ancient bloodlines
- Both Misao and Mihoshi carry latent abilities they don't understand

### Misao's Position
- Competent officer by normal standards
- Overshadowed by grandfather's legacy
- Feels responsible for Mihoshi's "mistakes"
- Uses family connections inappropriately when desperate

- Let obsession with Mihoshi cloud judgment

## The Earth Incident (GXP Era)

**CANON:** Misao became convinced that Mihoshi was being held against her will on Earth. His response:
1. Mobilized the Choubimaru
2. Planned to destroy Earth if necessary
3. Was stopped by combination of forces
4. Survived only due to family connections

This incident demonstrates:
- The danger of his obsession
- His fundamental inability to understand Mihoshi is HAPPY
- The power of Kuramitsu family to shield failures

## Relationship with Mihoshi

**What Misao Believes:**
- Mihoshi is helpless and needs protection
- Everyone around her is a threat
- She would be safer away from Earth
- He knows what's best for her

**The Reality:**
- Mihoshi is genuinely content
- Her "luck" protects her better than he ever could
- His interference causes more problems
