# Ryo-Ohki

## Basic Information
- **Species**: Cabbit (cat-rabbit hybrid artificial lifeform)
- **Creator**: Washu Hakubi
- **Age**: 700+ years (imprisoned with Ryoko)
- **Role**: Ryoko's partner, spaceship, and beloved companion

---

## Nature & Origins

### Creation
Ryo-Ohki is a unique artificial lifeform created by Washu, combining:
- A powerful entity called Masu
- Mineral-based life (crystalline structure)
- Originally designed as a purely crystalline spaceship

### The Cat-Rabbit Form
Ryo-Ohki gained her distinctive cabbit appearance when Ryoko merged ship components with a dying cat and rabbit belonging to Tenchi. This gave her animal characteristics and personality.

### Family Relations
- **Mother**: Washu Hakubi
- **Sister**: Ryoko Hakubi (and later, Fuku)
- **Close companions**: Sasami, Kenshi Masaki
- **Spouse**: Tenchi Masaki (in extended canon)

---

## Forms & Shapeshifting

### Cabbit Form (Primary)
- Small, cute animal resembling both cat and rabbit
- Brown/tan fur with long ears
- Expressive eyes
- This is her most natural and comfortable form

### Child Humanoid Form
- Achieved after merging with the Masu
- Appears as a young girl
- Usually wears similar clothing to Sasami
- More articulate than cabbit form

### Adult Humanoid Form
- Rarely used due to childlike mentality
- Difficulty walking bipedally
- Functionally capable but uncomfortable
- By GXP era, shows more fluency in this form

### Spaceship Form
- Powerful crystalline vessel
- Rests at a lake's bottom when not in use
- Sleek, organic design
- Can travel interstellar distances easily

---

## Personality & Communication

### Childlike Nature
- Innocent and playful demeanor
- Loves carrots above all else
- Seeks affection and attention
- Emotionally sensitive

### Communication
- Primarily communicates through "Miya!" (meows)
- According to novels, this is "high speed compressed language"
- By GXP series, humanoid form demonstrates conversational fluency
- Can understand complex concepts despite simple vocalizations

### Romantic Feelings
- Harbors romantic feelings toward Tenchi
- Her immaturity prevents genuine romance
- Competes with the other women in a childlike way
- Considered one of Tenchi's wives in extended canon

---

## Powers & Capabilities

### Without Gems
- Matches a third-generation Juraian starship
- Flight capability
- Energy projection
- Structural regeneration

### With Ryoko's Three Gems
- Can generate ten Light Hawk Wings
- Equivalent to Tsunami's power level
- Near-godlike capabilities
- Only achieved when fully synchronized with Ryoko

### As a Spaceship
- Interstellar travel
- Atmospheric entry/exit
- Combat maneuvering
- Serves as both vessel and computational unit

### Special Abilities
- Shapeshifting between forms
- Regeneration
- Bonding with partners
- Subspace connection

---

## Historical Significance

### The Dark Past
- Originally controlled by antagonist Kagato
- Under Kagato's control, she and Ryoko destroyed 28 planets
- Used as a weapon of terror for 5,000 years
- Had no choice in these actions

### Defeat by Yosho
- Prince Yosho of Jurai defeated Ryoko and Ryo-Ohki 700 years before the main narrative
- Ryoko was sealed in a cave
- Ryo-Ohki remained dormant near Earth's Masaki Shrine
- Awaited awakening for centuries

### Liberation
- Freed when Ryoko was accidentally released
- Reunited with her partner
- Now lives peacefully at the Masaki household

---

## Relationships

### With Ryoko
- Inseparable partners
- Share a deep psychic/emotional bond
- Ryoko is protective of Ryo-Ohki
- They were created as a pair

### With Sasami
- Close friends
- Sasami often cooks for her (especially carrots)
- Playmate and companion
- Shares childlike innocence

### With Tenchi
- Adores him completely
- Competes for his attention
- Loyal protector
- In extended canon, becomes one of his wives

### With Fuku
- Younger sister (also created by Washu)
- Teaching role in some interactions
- Fuku was designed to be compatible with her

### With Ken-Ohki
- Another cabbit (from Tenchi Universe)
- Ken-Ohki has romantic feelings for her
- This affects battles between Ryoko and Nagi

## Diet & Care

### Favorite Foods
- Carrots (absolute favorite)
- Carrot juice
- Carrot-based dishes
- Sasami's cooking

### Care Requirements
- Affection and attention
- Proper feeding
- Emotional support
- Connection to Ryoko

## Story Significance

Ryo-Ohki represents:
- The bond between partners (with Ryoko)
- Innocence and redemption (from weapon to family member)
- The "mascot" character of Tenchi Muyo
- Comic relief through her carrot obsession
- Hidden power beneath a cute exterior
- The possibility of peace after violence
