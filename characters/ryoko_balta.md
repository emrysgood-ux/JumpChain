# Ryoko Balta

## Basic Information
- **Full Name**: Ryoko Balta
- **Also Known As**: Erma (undercover identity)
- **Species**: Wau (shapeshifter)
- **Origin**: Daluma Guild / Balta Kingdom
- **Role**: Main character in Tenchi Muyo! GXP

---

## Background

### Pirate Origins
Ryoko Balta was "a beautiful space pirate of the Daluma Guild" who operated with considerable influence across the galaxy. Despite her infamy:
- She is "hardly the bloodthirsty pirate" her reputation suggests
- Demonstrates refinement and culture
- Expert in Japanese tea ceremony
- Skilled in cooking

### True Heritage
Unknown to her initially:
- She is actually a princess of the Balta Kingdom
- One of the five kingdoms of the Renza Federation
- Her royal status was hidden from her

---

## The Erma Identity

### Undercover Mission
Ryoko infiltrated the Galaxy Police Academy disguised as a Wau woman named Erma:
- Tasked with gathering intelligence about Seina Yamada
- Worked as a spy for the Daluma Guild
- Maintained a completely different appearance and personality

### Shapeshifting Capability
Her unique ability allows:
- Complete genetic restructuring
- Maintaining distinct personalities for each form
- Fooling security systems
- Extended infiltration operations

---

## Relationship with Seina

### Initial Mission
- Ordered to gather intelligence on Seina
- Later ordered to assassinate him
- This created a crisis of loyalty

### Falling in Love
Her encounter with Seina fundamentally altered her life:
- Developed genuine feelings across both personas
- As Erma, became close to him at the Academy
- As herself, was drawn to his kindness

### The Turning Point
When ordered to kill Seina:
- She renounced her pirate allegiances
- Seina gave her a simple star pendant
- She "dropped her disguise and told Seina the truth"
- Chose love over duty

---

## Personality & Skills

### Personality Traits
- Cultured and refined (unexpected for a pirate)
- Loyal once trust is established
- Conflicted between duty and heart (initially)
- Warm and caring toward those she loves

### Skills & Abilities
| Skill | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Shapeshifting | Complete genetic restructuring |
| Tea Ceremony | Japanese traditional arts |
| Cooking | Skilled chef |
| Combat | Pirate training |
| Espionage | Expert infiltrator |

### Reputation
Her cultured demeanor earned her devoted admirers throughout the Galaxy Police ranks, even before her true identity was known.

---

## GXP Career

### Joining the Galaxy Police
After revealing herself:
- Officially joined the GP as an officer
- Assigned to the Kamidake crew
- Later served on Kamidake II

### Crew Role
- Crew member aboard Seina's ship
- Combat operations
- Brings unique perspective from pirate background
- Knowledge of criminal operations useful for GP work

### Political Marriage
Married Seina in a political union:
- One of four women in the primary marriage
- Others: Kiriko Masaki, Amane Kaunaq, Neju Na Melmas
- Later joined by Renza Federation wives

---

## Discovery of Heritage

### Balta Kingdom Princess
Ryoko discovered she was royalty:
- Princess of the Balta Kingdom
- One of the five kingdoms of Renza Federation
- This added political dimensions to her marriage to Seina

### Implications
- Connected Seina to Renza Federation politics
- Bridged the gap between pirate world and legitimate power
- Added complexity to her identity

---

## The Two Ryokos

### Name Significance
She shares her name with Ryoko Hakubi:
- Different characters entirely
- Different origins (Balta vs. Washu's creation)
- Both associated with piracy
- Both became part of the extended Masaki family network

### Distinguishing Features
| Aspect | Ryoko Hakubi | Ryoko Balta |
|--------|--------------|-------------|
| Creator | Washu | Natural birth |
| Powers | Superhuman (gems) | Shapeshifting |
| Partner | Tenchi | Seina |
| Origin | Artificial | Royal |
| Series | Main OVA | GXP |

## Character Arc

### Transformation
1. **Pirate**: Loyal to Daluma Guild
2. **Spy**: Undercover as Erma
3. **Conflicted**: Falling for her target
4. **Redeemed**: Choosing love over mission
5. **Officer**: Legitimate GP career
6. **Princess**: Discovering true heritage
7. **Wife**: Part of Seina's family

### Themes
- Redemption through love
- Identity beyond roles
- Finding family in unexpected places
- The power of genuine connection

---

## Story Significance

Ryoko Balta represents:
- That pirates can find redemption
- Love transforming enemies into allies
- The complexity of identity and loyalty
- Connection between criminal underworld and legitimate society
- The GXP tradition of unlikely romances

---

## Notable Quotes (Thematic)

The moment she chose Seina over her mission demonstrates:
- Personal bonds over organizational loyalty
- The transformative power of being valued
- That a simple gift (the star pendant) meant more than pirate glory
