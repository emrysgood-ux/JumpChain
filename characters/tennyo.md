## Birth and Early Life (~1910-1980s)

Tennyo was born approximately 1910 to Kiyone Masaki and Nobuyuki Masaki at the Masaki Shrine. As the daughter of a quarter-Juraian mother and a nominally human father (Nobuyuki is actually descended from Juraian colonists), Tennyo inherited enough Juraian heritage to dramatically slow her aging.

### The Problem of Unchanging Youth

By the 1960s-1970s, Tennyo's unchanged appearance became untenable:
- Villagers who remembered her as a young woman still saw her as a young woman
- Her father Nobuyuki had visibly aged while she hadn't
- Rumors and questions became unavoidable

**Resolution:** Tennyo left Earth, establishing residence at the Galaxy Academy where her grandmother Airi serves as Chairwoman. Her departure was staged as "going abroad for work" ‚Äî technically true.

## The Burden of Outliving

Tennyo faces the same tragedy as her grandfather Yosho: watching mortal-seeming family members age and die while she remains young. She:
- Watched her mother age and die at 248 (young by Juraian standards but still aging)
- Will likely watch her father Nobuyuki die of old age
- Knows Tenchi may face the same aging issues unless he bonds with a Royal Tree
