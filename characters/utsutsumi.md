## The Legendary Marriage

### How They Met
When Seto was approximately 4,500 years old and Utsutsumi was a 9-year-old academy student, he tripped and fell on her while she was napping in a bush.

### The "Proposal"
Seto ‚Äî already the feared Devil Princess ‚Äî feigned weakness and accused the child of groping her. She gave him a choice: **marriage or death**.

Young Utsutsumi, in what he claims was "choosing the harder route out of habit," agreed to marry her.

### The Reality
Despite the absurd beginning:
- They are genuinely devoted to each other after 4,500 years
- Utsutsumi is one of the few beings who can calm Seto
- Their relationship evolved from her game into real partnership
- He provides the steady anchor to her chaos

## House Kamiki

Utsutsumi leads House Kamiki, one of the four great houses of the Juraian Empire:
- Controls significant political and economic power
- Maintains vast estates and fleets
- Known for producing skilled administrators and warriors
- Second only to House Masaki (the royal house) in prestige

### His Leadership Style
- Practical rather than flashy
- Delegates well (often to Seto, officially or not)
- Maintains stability while his wife creates chaos
- Respected by subordinates for fairness

## Role in Juraian Politics

Officially, Utsutsumi is a major political figure as head of House Kamiki. Unofficially:
- Seto handles most political maneuvering
- He manages the house's day-to-day operations
- Provides legitimacy and stability
- Acts as moderating influence on Seto's schemes

### The Unspoken Agreement
Everyone in Juraian politics knows:
- Cross House Kamiki, you cross Seto
- But Utsutsumi is the reasonable one to negotiate with
- He can sometimes talk Seto down from her more extreme plans
- Sometimes.

## Relationship with Seto

After 4,500 years, their marriage defies simple description:

**What He Provides:**
- Stability to her chaos
- Patience for her schemes
- A home to return to
- Someone who sees past the "Devil Princess" mask

**What She Provides:**
- Excitement (whether he wants it or not)
- Protection (nothing threatens House Kamiki)
- Genuine affection (in her way)
- A partnership of equals (eventually)

**The Truth:**
Utsutsumi genuinely loves his wife. He simply expresses it through endurance rather than grand gestures. And Seto, for all her manipulation of others, has never truly manipulated him ‚Äî she respects him too much.
