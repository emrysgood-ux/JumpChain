## 1. CORE IDENTITY

### 1.1 — Origin

Kagato was created by **Naja Akara**, Washu's former research partner, using genetic material from Washu herself. He was designed as a servant/assistant but developed beyond his intended parameters.

**Key Points:**
- Contains Washu's genetic material
- Not Washu's "son" in any emotional sense
- Developed independent will and ambition
- Eventually turned against his creators

### 1.2 — Physical Description

- Tall, thin humanoid male
- Long silver-white hair
- Pale skin
- Cold, calculating eyes
- Elegant appearance
- Wears formal, robe-like attire

### 1.3 — Personality

**Dominant Traits:**
- Ruthlessly ambitious
- Coldly intelligent
- Patient (waited millennia for plans)
- Views others as tools or obstacles
- Obsessed with power acquisition

**Psychological Profile:**
- Sees himself as superior to "natural" beings
- No empathy or emotional connections
- Views Washu as both creator and obstacle
- Despises being considered a "creation"

## 3. HISTORY

### 3.1 — Creation

- Created by Naja Akara using Washu's genetic material
- Originally intended as assistant/servant
- Developed beyond parameters
- Betrayed creators when strong enough

### 3.2 — Rise to Power

- Captured Washu; imprisoned her in crystal
- Took control of Soja (Washu's ship)
- Enslaved Ryoko using the control gems
- Used Ryoko to attack Jurai (~1300 AD)
- This attack is what brought Yosho to Earth

### 3.3 — The Jurai Attack

**Sequence of Events:**
1. Kagato sent Ryoko to attack Jurai
2. Massive destruction to the empire
3. Yosho pursued Ryoko to Earth
4. Yosho sealed Ryoko in the cave
5. Yosho took the gems (now in Tenchi-ken)
6. Kagato lost direct control of Ryoko

### 3.4 — Waiting Period (1300 AD - 2001)

After losing Ryoko, Kagato:
- Kept Washu imprisoned
- Searched for the gems' location
- Developed other power sources
- Eventually tracked the gems to Earth

### 3.5 — Canon Death (2001)

**OVA Episodes 5-6:**
1. Kagato arrives on Earth seeking gems
2. Kidnaps Ryoko to regain control
3. Confronts Tenchi on Soja
4. Tenchi manifests Light Hawk Wings
5. Tenchi kills Kagato with the Master Key
6. Washu is freed from imprisonment

## 4. RELATIONSHIPS

### 4.1 — Washu (Creator)

- Views her as obstacle to his ambitions
- Kept her imprisoned to prevent interference
- Fears her full Chousin power
- Does not respect her despite using her technology

### 4.2 — Ryoko (Slave)

- Views her purely as a weapon
- No emotional connection
- Controlled her through gems and psychology
- Her freedom from him is a plot point

### 4.3 — Tenchi (Killer)

- Underestimated him completely
- Did not expect independent Light Hawk Wing generation
- His death by Tenchi's hand is karmic
