# Seina Yamada

## Basic Information
- **Full Name**: Seina Yamada
- **Species**: Human (Earth/enhanced)
- **Age**: 15 (at series start)
- **Origin**: Earth (same village as Tenchi)
- **Role**: Protagonist of Tenchi Muyo! GXP

---

## The Worst Luck in the Universe

### Nature of His Luck
Seina Yamada possesses "the worst luck in the universe," described in novels as a "biased probability." This isn't simple bad luckâ€”it's a cosmic-scale phenomenon:
- Disasters follow him constantly
- Accidents happen wherever he goes
- Injuries are a daily occurrence
- His mere presence causes chaos

### The Paradox
Rather than being purely detrimental, his extraordinary misfortune has an unexpected benefit:
- Space pirates are inexplicably drawn to him
- His bad luck functions like a pirate magnet
- The Galaxy Police strategically exploits this for operations
- He becomes their most effective pirate trap

## Background

### Earth Life
- Lived in the same village as Tenchi Masaki
- Constantly injured throughout childhood
- Everyone around him experienced collateral damage
- Childhood friend of Kiriko Masaki

### Accidental Recruitment
- Detective Amane Kaunaq mistakenly believed he was Tenchi Masaki
- She gave him a GP recruitment application
- His dog ate the rejection notice
- He was recruited into the Galaxy Police by accident

---

## Galaxy Police Career

### Training
- Attended the GP Academy
- Survived despite his luck (barely)
- Made friends and connections
- Demonstrated unexpected competence

### Command
- Became captain of the Kamidake
- Later commanded the Kamidake II
- Ship designed by Washu
- Bonded with Fuku (cabbit power source)

### Kamidake II Capabilities
- Designed by Washu specifically for Seina
- Uses Fuku as main power source
- Self-upgrading and adapting
- When combined with Mikagami and Mizuki, can generate Light Hawk Wings

---

## Royal Status

### The Idol Discovery
Seina discovered a billion-year-old mecha called the "Idol":
- Powered by a first-generation Royal Tree Seed
- One of the most ancient artifacts in existence
- Connected to the Renza Federation's origins

### Royal Tree Bond
By bonding with this ancient seed:
- Became third in line to the Jurai throne
- Gained status equivalent to a first-generation tree partner
- Fundamentally altered his destiny
- Created political connections across the galaxy

## Character Development

### From Unlucky Boy to Hero
- Starts as a perpetual victim of circumstance
- Grows into a capable commander
- Learns to work with his luck rather than against it
- Becomes a galactic political figure

### Personality Traits
- Kind-hearted despite suffering
- Resilient (has to be)
- Humble despite achievements
- Genuinely cares for his crew and wives

---

## Powers & Abilities

### Body Enhancement
- Received standard GP enhancement procedures
- Lifespan extended to ~2,000 years
- Enhanced strength and endurance

### Luck Manipulation (Passive)
- Can't control his luck
- But it does protect him in battle (usually)
- Pirates are drawn to him (useful for ambushes)

### Command Abilities
- Effective ship captain
- Inspires loyalty in crew
- Makes good tactical decisions (when luck allows)

### Light Hawk Wings (Combined)
- Through the Kamidake II's merged configuration
- When combined with other ships
- Can access this ultimate power

## Story Significance

Seina represents:
- The ordinary person thrust into extraordinary circumstances
- That apparent weaknesses can become strengths
- Connection between Earth and the wider galaxy
- Political bridge between multiple powers (Jurai, GP, Renza)
- The harem comedy tradition of Tenchi Muyo
