## 1. CORE IDENTITY

### 1.1 — Unique Status

Funaho is one of the only HUMANS to be accepted into the Juraian royal family. Her integration was politically controversial but ultimately successful, establishing a precedent that would later apply to others.

### 1.2 — Origin

- Born on Earth
- Details of her early life are limited in canon
- Encountered Azusa before he became Emperor
- Fell in love; was brought to Jurai
- Bond with Royal Tree Mizuho extended her lifespan

### 1.3 — Physical Description

- Appears as elegant human woman
- Japanese features
- Long dark hair (traditional style)
- Graceful, composed bearing
- Appears ~30s-40s (actual age much greater)

### 1.4 — Personality

**Dominant Traits:**
- Gentle and diplomatic
- Quiet strength
- Patient and understanding
- Deeply loves her family
- Bridge between cultures

**Psychological Profile:**
- Adapted to alien culture successfully
- Maintains connection to Earth heritage
- Grieves Yosho's "loss" quietly
- Supportive partner to Azusa

## 2. RELATIONSHIPS

### 2.1 — Azusa (Husband)

- Genuine love match despite politics
- Balances his sternness with grace
- Supports his rule quietly
- Their relationship survived controversy

### 2.2 — Yosho (Son)

- Deeply loves him
- Grieves his "exile" on Earth
- Respects his choices even if she misses him
- Would welcome contact if possible

### 2.3 — Misaki (Co-Wife)

- Friendly relationship
- Share duty of supporting Azusa
- Different personalities complement each other
- Not rivals despite polygamous arrangement

### 2.4 — Grandchildren

- Has never met Tenchi (as of 1989)
- Would be overjoyed to know of his existence
- Would want to visit Earth

## 3. SIGNIFICANCE

### 3.1 — Political Importance

Her acceptance established:
- Humans CAN integrate into Jurai
- Royal Tree bonds CAN work with non-Juraians
- Love matches CAN transcend politics
- Earth IS connected to Jurai (through her and Yosho)

### 3.2 — Thematic Importance

Funaho represents:
- Bridge between worlds
- Acceptance of outsiders
- Love transcending boundaries
- Human potential in cosmic context
