## TRUE NATURE (Hidden)

### The Chousin
Three sister goddesses who created the 22-dimensional universe:
- **Washu** — Sealed her memories, descended to mortal form
- **Tsunami** — Bonded with Sasami, manifests through Royal Trees
- **Tokimi** — Remained in higher dimensions

### Why She Descended
- The Chousin seek a being who transcends even them
- Washu chose to experience existence from within
- Sealed her own memories and powers
- Genuinely doesn't remember being a goddess (mostly)

### The Experiment
All of Tenchi Muyo is the Chousin experiment to create/find a transcendent being. Tenchi is the successful result.

## HISTORY

### Ancient Past
- Created universe with sisters
- Descended, sealed memories
- Became mortal scientist
- Created Ryoko from her own DNA + Masu
- Had relationship with Naja Akara
- Naja disappeared, Kagato appeared (clone)

### Imprisonment
- Kagato turned on Washu
- Imprisoned her for ~5,000 years
- Stole Ryoko, used as weapon
- Washu trapped in subspace pocket

### Freedom (Year 13)
- Kagato comes to Earth
- Tenchi defeats Kagato
- Washu freed from imprisonment
- Joins household

## THE SHELDON INVESTIGATION

### What She Detects (Year 13+)
When Washu scans Sheldon:
- Unknown cosmic energy signature
- Not Chousin-derived
- Not catalogued anywhere
- "Another god's fingerprints"

### Her Conclusions
1. Sheldon is touched by something outside their experiment
2. Lambda Power predates or parallels Chousin creation
3. Cosmos operates in dimensions they don't control
4. "He's not from here. I mean REALLY not from here."

### The Investigation Arc
| Phase | Action |
|-------|--------|
| 1 | Detection (passive scans) |
| 2 | Curiosity (active observation) |
| 3 | Testing (subtle experiments) |
| 4 | Conclusion (accepts mystery) |

### What She Discovers
- Lambda Power exists
- Different from LHW entirely
- Nurturing, not combat-focused
- Source is "maternal cosmic entity"
- Chooses NOT to tell Sheldon about Lambda Infusion

### Why She Keeps Secrets
- Some things are better not known
- His cooking works BECAUSE he doesn't know
- Scientific curiosity satisfied
- Respects the mystery
- "A puzzle worth centuries"
