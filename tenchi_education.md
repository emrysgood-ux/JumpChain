## JAPANESE EDUCATION SYSTEM

### Structure

| Level | Japanese | Duration | Ages |
|-------|----------|----------|------|
| Kindergarten | 幼稚園 | 2-3 years | 3-6 |
| Elementary | 小学校 | 6 years | 6-12 |
| Middle School | 中学校 | 3 years | 12-15 |
| High School | 高等学校 | 3 years | 15-18 |

### School Year
- Starts: April
- Ends: March
- Age at start: Turn 6 by April 1 of entry year

## YEAR-BY-YEAR DETAIL

### Year 1: Kindergarten 年中 (1989-90)

**School Name:** Local yōchien (assume village kindergarten)

**Schedule:**
- Mornings only (typical for kindergarten)
- Walk to/from (Katsuhito or Sheldon)
- Home by early afternoon

**Sheldon's Role:**
- Sometimes walks him to school
- Prepares bento
- Afternoon activities
- Homework? (minimal at this age)

**Developmental Focus:**
- Social skills
- Basic counting
- Hiragana introduction
- Play-based learning

---

### Year 2: Kindergarten 年長 (1990-91)

**Final Kindergarten Year**

**Developmental Focus:**
- Preparing for elementary
- More structured learning
- Hiragana mastery
- Number recognition

**Story Elements:**
- Day 366 revelation (Sheldon)
- Tenchi unaware of cosmic truth
- Normal childhood continues

---

### Year 3: Elementary 1st Grade (1991-92)

**Major Transition**

**School Details:**
- Full day schedule
- Walking to school (group)
- Randoseru (leather backpack)
- School uniform

**Curriculum:**
- Japanese (hiragana, katakana)
- Math basics
- Life Studies (生活科)
- Music, Art, PE

**Sheldon's Role:**
- Homework help begins
- School lunch vs. bento
- After-school care
- Reading practice

---

### Year 4-5: Elementary 2nd-3rd Grade (1992-94)

**Consolidation Phase**

**Academic Progress:**
- Kanji introduction (Year 4)
- More complex math
- Science begins (Year 5)
- Social studies begins (Year 5)

**Scene Opportunities:**
- Homework struggles
- School friends mentioned
- Class events
- Parent-teacher days (Nobuyuki attends)

---

### Year 6-8: Elementary 4th-6th Grade (1994-97)

**Pre-Adolescence**

**Academic Demands:**
- Increasing homework
- Kanji accumulation (~1,000 by end)
- More abstract concepts
- Group projects

**Tenchi's Character:**
- Growing independence
- Questions about family
- Training with grandfather
- Cave curiosity grows

**Sheldon's Role:**
- Advanced homework help
- Life conversations
- Cooking lessons more serious
- Confidant role emerging

---

### Year 9: Middle School 1st Year (1997-98)

**Major Transition**

**Changes:**
- New school (larger, farther)
- Subject teachers (not homeroom all day)
- Club activities (部活)
- School uniform change

**Typical Schedule:**
- 8:30 AM - 3:30 PM classes
- Club activities until 5-6 PM
- More homework

**Tenchi Likely Joins:**
- Kendo club? (grandfather's influence)
- Or nature/science club?

---

### Year 10-11: Middle School 2nd-3rd (1998-2000)

**Adolescence Full**

**Academic Pressure:**
- High school entrance exams (Year 11)
- Juku (cram school) common (does Tenchi attend?)
- Serious study time

**Tenchi's Development:**
- Identity formation
- Growing strength
- More serious training
- Questions deeper

**Year 11 Specific:**
- March: Entrance exams
- Choice of high school
- Stress period

---

### Year 12: High School 1st Year (2000-01)

**Young Adulthood**

**High School Details:**
- Entrance ceremony April
- New peer group
- More independence
- After-school activities

**Tenchi at 15-16:**
- Nearly adult height
- Strong from training
- Curious about cave
- Unaware of destiny

**Final "Quiet" School Year:**
- Normal high school life
- Summer 2001 = canon begins
- Everything changes

---

### Year 13: High School 2nd Year (2001-02)

**CANON BEGINS**

**Summer 2001:**
- Releases Ryoko
- Life transforms
- School becomes secondary
- New "education" begins
