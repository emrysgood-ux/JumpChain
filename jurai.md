## THE ROYAL TREES

### Tsunami-no-ki
- Original tree, goddess incarnate
- Source of all Royal Trees
- Located on Jurai capital
- Most sacred site in empire

### Tree Generations
| Gen | Capabilities | Political Weight |
|-----|--------------|------------------|
| 1st | 3 LHW, full offense | Imperial family only |
| 2nd | 3 LHW, need override | High nobility |
| 3rd | 1 LHW, paired | Military standard |
| 4th+ | No LHW | Administrative |

### Tree Bonding
- Trees CHOOSE their partners
- Cannot be forced or transferred
- Grants longevity
- Defines political standing

## RELATIONSHIP WITH EARTH

### Official Status
- "Undeveloped world"
- Protected by obscurity
- No formal contact
- Technically Jurai territory

### Actual Situation
- Yosho exiled there 700 years
- Funaho (1st Empress) originated there
- Multiple Jurai descendants live there
- Masaki family = Jurai royalty in hiding

### Why No Intervention?
- Yosho's exile respected (mostly)
- Political complications
- Earth genuinely backwater
- Tsunami's indirect protection

## YOSHO'S SITUATION

### His Status
- First Crown Prince (abdicated)
- Officially "pursuing Ryoko"
- Actually exiled by choice
- Still has legal claims

### Why He Left
- Arranged marriage to Ayeka (half-sister)
- "Half-breed" discrimination
- Desired freedom
- Kagato attack provided excuse

### Current Relationship
- Azusa wants him back
- Funaho supports his choice
- Political factions divided
