## KNOWLEDGE STATES

### What Sheldon KNOWS:
- He died (Alaska, cascade failure, Maria Chen's face)
- His body is different (no scars, younger, restored)
- Documents say he is Tsukino Sheldon
- Japanese language is fluent (surprise to him)
- Family backstory per documents/letter
- He has some kind of power (Day 1 test)
- Power exhausts him completely when used

### What Sheldon DOES NOT KNOW:
- Anything about Tenchi Muyo universe
- Katsuhito's true nature (700+ year old Juraian prince)
- What's in the cave (Ryoko, sealed)
- Why he's here
- Who sent him
- That it's Sailor Cosmos
- The significance of the name "Tsukino"

## MULTI-POV PROTOCOL

When using multiple perspectives on the same scene:
1. Use **CHRONOLOGICAL INTERLEAVING** (Law 36 Decision)
2. Each POV header clearly marked (e.g., "KATSUHITO POV")
3. POV sections alternate in timeline order
4. No information leakage between perspectives
