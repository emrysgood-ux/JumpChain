## WRITING NOTES FOR PROSE

### Sheldon's POV Restrictions

When writing Sheldon observing Tenchi:

**ALLOWED:**
- Physical descriptions (black hair, brown eyes, child build)
- Behavioral observations (energetic, curious, stubborn)
- Age-appropriate interactions
- Normal child psychology

**FORBIDDEN:**
- Any knowledge of Tenchi's cosmic significance
- Recognition of Juraian heritage
- Understanding of Light Hawk Wings
- Awareness of the Chousin experiment
- Any cosmic or science-fiction terminology

### Behavioral Consistency (Age 4-5)

- Short attention span (5-10 minutes max)
- Physical energy that needs outlet
- Nap requirements (still napping at 4)
- Emotional volatility (quick to laugh, quick to cry)
- Attachment behaviors (clinging when scared)
- Bedtime resistance typical of age
- Picky eating (developmentally normal)

### Voice Consistency

- Simple vocabulary
- Short sentences
- Constant questions
- Interruptions
- Topic changes mid-sentence
- Mispronunciations (age-appropriate)
- Repetition for emphasis
