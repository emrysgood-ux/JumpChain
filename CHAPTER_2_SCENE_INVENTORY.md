**MAJOR EVENT:** Community work party (結/yui tradition)

### COMMUNITY WORK PARTY MECHANICS

**The Yui Tradition:**
- Mutual aid obligation in Japanese rural communities
- Those helped must help others in turn
- Food provided by receiving household
- Work from dawn to dusk
- Social as much as practical

**Participants (15-20 villagers):**
1. Nakamura Takeshi (council head) - organizing
2. Tanaka family representatives (3)
3. Yamada crew (4, already present)
4. Endo family (2 neighbors)
