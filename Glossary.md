## JAPANESE TERMS

### Architectural/Domestic

| Term | Reading | Meaning | Usage |
|------|---------|---------|-------|
| **Engawa** | えんがわ | Traditional wooden veranda | Tsukino house exterior feature |
| **Fusuma** | ふすま | Sliding door (opaque) | Interior room dividers |
| **Genkan** | げんかん | Entrance hall | Shoe removal area |
| **Ofuro** | おふろ | Traditional bath | Tsukino house bathroom |
| **Shoji** | しょうじ | Sliding door (paper) | Light-filtering screens |
| **Tatami** | たたみ | Woven floor mat | Room size measurement (6-mat = standard room) |
| **Tokonoma** | とこのま | Display alcove | Decorative niche in main room |

### Religious/Shrine Terms

| Term | Reading | Meaning | Usage |
|------|---------|---------|-------|
| **Jinja** | じんじゃ | Shinto shrine | Masaki Shrine |
| **Kami** | かみ | Spirit/deity | Shinto concept |
| **Kannushi** | かんぬし | Shinto priest | Katsuhito's role |
| **Miko** | みこ | Shrine maiden | NOT present at Masaki Shrine |
| **Temizu-ya** | てみずや | Purification fountain | At shrine entrance |
| **Torii** | とりい | Shrine gate | Multiple along 320 steps |

### Honorifics

| Term | Reading | Meaning | Usage |
|------|---------|---------|-------|
| **-chan** | ちゃん | Affectionate diminutive | Ten-chan (Tenchi) |
| **-jii** | じい | Grandfather | Jii-chan (Katsuhito) |
| **-san** | さん | Respectful (Mr./Ms.) | Standard address |
| **-sensei** | せんせい | Teacher/expert | Professional respect |

### Time/Calendar

| Term | Reading | Meaning | Usage |
|------|---------|---------|-------|
| **Obon** | おぼん | Festival of the Dead | August 13-16; Ryoko's release date |

### Family Terms

| Term | Reading | Meaning | Usage |
|------|---------|---------|-------|

## TENCHI MUYO TERMINOLOGY

### Species/Biology

| Term | Definition | Notes |
|------|------------|-------|
| **Chousin** | The three sister goddesses who created the multiverse | Washu, Tsunami, Tokimi |
| **Juraian** | Species from planet Jurai; distinguished by Royal Tree bonds | Long-lived, powerful |
| **Juraian Royal** | Tree-bonded Juraian with enhanced powers | Yosho, Ayeka, Sasami |
| **Juraian Unbonded** | Juraian without tree bond | Kiyone Masaki (248-year lifespan) |
| **Kami Tenchi** | The ultimate being sought by the Chousin | Dormant within Tenchi |

### Technology/Powers

| Term | Definition | Notes |
|------|------------|-------|
| **Illusion barrier** | Technique to disguise appearance | Yosho uses to appear elderly |
| **Light Hawk Wings** | Juraian power manifestation | Tenchi: 3 wings (potentially unlimited) |
| **Royal Tree** | Sentient Juraian ship-trees | Funaho is Yosho's tree |
| **Ship form** | Royal Trees can transform into spacecraft | Funaho CANNOT (rooted) |

### Locations

| Term | Definition | Notes |
|------|------------|-------|
| **Jurai** | Homeworld of Juraian civilization | Yosho's birthplace |
| **Galaxy Academy** | Interstellar educational institution | Airi's location |
| **Sōja** | Kagato's ship | Where Washu is sealed |

## STORY-SPECIFIC TERMINOLOGY

### Sheldon's Constructed History

| Term | Definition | Notes |
|------|------------|-------|
| **Constructed history** | Sheldon's implanted Japanese family background | Created by Cosmos |
| **Harmonization** | Process by which Sheldon's soul synced with local identity | Not "replacement" |
| **Trigger date** | When Jumper memories unlock | Story start for Sheldon |

### POV Restrictions

| Forbidden Term | Acceptable Replacement |
|----------------|----------------------|
| Benefactor | "the system," "the presence," "whatever brought him here" |
| Cosmos | "his benefactor," "the presence" |
| JumpChain/Jump | "his situation," "this second chance" |
| Lambda | Describe effect, not name |
| Perk | "ability," "skill," "gift" |

### Location-Specific

| Term | Definition | Location |
|------|------------|----------|
| **320 steps** | Exact count of shrine stairs | Masaki Shrine (based on Tarojinja) |
| **Funaho** | Yosho's Royal Tree | Beyond main shrine building |
