# The Three Gems of Washu

## Overview
The Three Gems are artifacts containing the sealed powers of Washu, one of the Choushin goddesses. They serve as power sources, control mechanisms, and plot-critical objects throughout the Tenchi Muyo series.

---

## Origin

### Washu's Decision
When Washu chose to seal her godly powers:
- She divided her divine essence into three gems
- Sealed her memories of being a Choushin
- Wanted to learn and experience from scratch
- The gems contain her goddess-level power

### Purpose
- Allow Washu to exist as a "mortal" scientist
- Provide power source for her creations
- Serve as keys to her true nature
- Can restore her godhood if reunited

---

## Physical Description

### Appearance
- Three spherical gems
- Distinct coloring (often depicted as red/orange)
- Glow with internal energy
- Size varies in depictions

### Location During Series
The gems were distributed to Ryoko:
- One on each wrist (bracelet-like)
- One on her neck/collar
- Physically attached to Ryoko's body

---

## Powers & Capabilities

### For Ryoko
When Ryoko possesses the gems:

| Gems | Capability | Power Level |
|------|------------|-------------|
| 0 | Baseline powers | Reduced |
| 1 | Standard abilities | Functional |
| 2 | Enhanced powers | Significant |
| 3 | Full power | 10 Light Hawk Wings with Ryo-Ohki |

### Specific Abilities Granted
- Energy projection
- Phasing/intangibility
- Flight
- Teleportation
- Superhuman strength
- Regeneration
- Matter manipulation

### For Ryo-Ohki
With all three gems, Ryo-Ohki can:
- Generate 10 Light Hawk Wings
- Match Tsunami's power level
- Become one of the most powerful vessels in existence

---

## Control Function

### Kagato's Exploitation
Kagato used the gems to control Ryoko:
- The gems were linked to Ryoko's creation
- Whoever controlled the gems controlled her
- This is how Kagato enslaved her for 5,000 years
- She had no choice in destroying 28 planets

### Breaking the Control
When Kagato was defeated:
- His control over the gems ended
- Ryoko gained autonomous control
- She was freed from external manipulation

---

## History

### Creation Era
- Washu sealed her powers into the gems
- Used them to create Ryoko and Ryo-Ohki
- The gems became integral to her creations

### Kagato Era (5,000 years)
- Kagato imprisoned Washu
- Used the gems to control Ryoko
- Weaponized them for destruction
- Sought additional power from Jurai

### Liberation
- Tenchi's intervention ended Kagato's reign
- Washu was freed
- Ryoko gained freedom and gem control

### Current Status
- Ryoko possesses all three gems
- Washu remains in her sealed, mortal-ish state
- The gems function as Ryoko's power source

---

## Relationship to Washu

### Sealed Power
The gems represent:
- Washu's divine essence
- Her choice to experience mortality
- A safety mechanism for restoration
- The price of her scientific curiosity

### Restoration Possibility
If Washu chose to reclaim the gems:
- She could restore her full Choushin powers
- Her memories would return
- She would transcend her mortal limitations
- She has chosen not to do this

### The Scientist's Choice
Washu prefers:
- Learning through experience
- The joy of discovery
- Relationships with her "children" (Ryoko, Ryo-Ohki)
- Being part of the Masaki household

---

## Story Significance

### Power Scale Marker
The gems establish:
- Ryoko's power ceiling (10 Light Hawk Wings possible)
- The difference between limited and full Ryoko
- Why Ryoko was feared as a weapon
- The connection between Washu and her creations

### Control & Freedom Theme
- Represent slavery when controlled by Kagato
- Represent freedom when Ryoko controls them
- Parallel themes of autonomy throughout series

### Washu's Character
- Show she chose limits for herself
- Demonstrate her love of learning over power
- Connect her to her "daughters"
- Represent potential she deliberately set aside

---

## Technical Specifications

### Energy Output
- Single gem: Substantial (maintains Ryoko's powers)
- All three: Goddess-level (10 Light Hawk Wings)
- Combined with Ryo-Ohki: Maximum conventional power

### Durability
- Nearly indestructible
- Survived millennia of use
- Can withstand massive energy discharge
- Tied to Washu's existence

### Attunement
- Primarily attuned to Ryoko
- Can respond to Washu
- Connection to Ryo-Ohki
- Others cannot simply use them

---

## For Story Use

### Power Reference
When writing about Ryoko's capabilities:
- Note how many gems she has access to
- Full power requires all three
- Kagato-era Ryoko was at full power but controlled
- Modern Ryoko chooses how much power to use

### Thematic Use
The gems can represent:
- Freedom vs. control
- The cost of power
- Choice and autonomy
- The relationship between creator and creation
