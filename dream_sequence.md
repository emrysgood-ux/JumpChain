## STAGE SUMMARIES

### Stage 1: The Echo (Day 14, April 17, 1989)

**Preceding Context:**
- Two weeks into new life
- Establishing routines at shrine
- Pattern of easy sleep noted (abnormal for Sheldon)
- Warmth in chest present but unexamined

**Scene Placement:** Night scene, end of Day 14 activities

**Key Elements:**
- Shape at edge of perception
- Words unclear, fragmentary
- Dismissed as meaningless dream

**Aftermath:** Returns to normal routine; dismisses dream

---

### Stage 2: Soft Contact (Day 75, June 17, 1989)

**Preceding Context:**
- A particularly good day required before this dream
- Perfect rice at breakfast (Katsuhito noticed)
- Helped Tenchi with school project (plant drawings)
- Excellent dinner
- Peaceful evening on engawa, Tenchi fell asleep on his shoulder
- Genuine peace achieved for first time since death

**Scene Placement:** Night scene following the good day (write the good day first)

**Key Elements:**
- Closer presence, vast but personally focused
- Felt rather than seen
- First emotional breakthrough

**Aftermath:** Wakes with tears; stops analyzing the warmth; accepts being watched over

---

### Stage 3: Identity Pressure (Day 130, August 10, 1989)

**Preceding Context:**
- Three days of Obon preparations (Days 127-130)
- First Obon since Kiyone Masaki's death
- Thinking about mortality, veils, the spaces between
- Physical exhaustion from ritual work
- Emotional weight of helping household grieve

**Scene Placement:** Night scene during Obon preparation arc

**Key Elements:**
- Features almost discernible
- First real dialogue
- Learns he was dying, she saved him
- Cannot dismiss anymore

**Aftermath:** Wakes shaking; carries cosmic knowledge into festival

---

### Stage 4: The First Son (Day 145, August 25, 1989)

**Preceding Context:**
- Post-Obon emotional processing complete
- Three nights of intensified dreams since Obon ended
- Actively wanting answers now
- Goes to sleep reaching for connection (not waiting for it)

**Scene Placement:** Night scene, mid-late August after Obon arc concludes

**Key Elements:**
- Clear enough to see fully
- Identity explicitly stated: "My first son"
- Extended conversation possible
- "I am her son" accepted

**Aftermath:** Identity crystallizes; warmth now understood

---

### Stage 5: The Harem Complaint (Day 152, September 1, 1989)

**Preceding Context:**
- Week of observations about universe characteristics
- Noticed: sealed cave, ancient grandfather, destined boy, gender ratio
- Frustration fully formed before sleep
- Actively bringing complaint to the dream

**Scene Placement:** Night scene, first week of September

**Key Elements:**
- Fully present, can blush
- Comedy without breaking truth
- "You put me in a harem anime"
- Cosmos's embarrassment and recovery

**Aftermath:** Genre-aware; relationship deepened through comedy; collaboration planned

---

### Stage 6: The Threshold of Creation (Day 366, April 3, 1990)

**Preceding Context:**
- First anniversary of arrival
- Full year of healing complete
- All prior dream stages have built to this moment
- One year of patience from Cosmos finally rewarded

**Scene Placement:** Night scene; serves as Year 1 conclusion / Year 2 opener

**Key Elements:**
- Complete manifestation, all barriers gone
- Full revelation of JumpChain mechanics
- "Mom" relationship explicit
- Freedom gift granted
- No obligations established

**Aftermath:** All perks unlocked; full knowledge holder; Year 2 begins
