### Core Traits
- **Analytical:** Processes emotions through logic first
- **Patient:** Comfortable with waiting, observation
- **Meticulous:** Attention to detail ("like your father")
- **Reserved:** Processes internally before speaking
- **Adaptive:** Survival instinct overrides panic

### Coping Mechanisms
- **Work:** Buries grief in productivity
