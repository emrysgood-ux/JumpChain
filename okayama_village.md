## VILLAGE PERCEPTION OF SHRINE

### What Villagers Know
- Ancient shrine on mountain
- Masaki family maintains it
- Old priest is wise, private
- Grandson Tenchi visits/lives there
- Spiritual but not tourist destination

### What Villagers Don't Know
- Priest is 700-year-old alien
- Tree is sentient spaceship
- Demon sealed in cave
- Any cosmic significance

### Relationship with Shrine
- Respectful distance
- Visit for prayers, omamori
- Major festivals attended
- Don't pry into family matters

## SHELDON'S INTEGRATION

### Village Perception of Sheldon
- "Tsukino-san" — inherited old house
- Quiet, polite foreigner (unusual but accepted)
- Excellent cook (shares sometimes)
- Helps at shrine
- Good with children

### How He's Accepted
- Katsuhito vouches for him
- Doesn't cause trouble
- Contributes to community
- Respects local customs
- Time heals strangeness
