## The Bad Luck Phenomenon

Seina Yamada possesses the most extreme case of bad luck ever documented. This is NOT merely superstition ‚Äî it is a measurable probability distortion field.

### How It Manifests
- Accidents constantly happen around him
- Objects fall, break, malfunction in his presence
- Weather turns bad when he's outside
- Vehicles crash, machines fail, structures collapse
- **CRITICAL:** The bad luck affects his SURROUNDINGS, not him directly ‚Äî he usually survives

### The Hidden Truth
What appears as "bad luck" is actually:
- A probability manipulation field of unknown origin
- Possibly a mutation, possibly something stranger
- The Galaxy Police eventually weaponizes this: Seina as pirate bait
- When Seina is assigned as decoy, pirates ALWAYS appear ‚Äî and ALWAYS suffer disasters

*üìù Seina's "curse" is actually the galaxy's most effective anti-pirate weapon.*

## Galaxy Police Career (GXP Era)

### Recruitment
Seina was accidentally recruited into the Galaxy Police when Amane Kaunaq crashed near his home (his bad luck pulled her ship down). The GP quickly realized his potential:
- His probability field attracts pirates
- Pirates who attack him suffer catastrophic "accidents"
- He's the perfect decoy/bait

### Rise Through Ranks
Despite being recruited as bait, Seina's genuine heroism and the loyalty he inspires led to:
- Rapid promotion
- Command of experimental ship Kamidake
- Marriage into multiple noble houses
- Becoming one of GP's most effective (if unconventional) assets

## The Seto Connection

Seto Kamiki Jurai became intensely interested in Seina because:
1. His probability manipulation might have cosmic origins
2. He could be useful against her enemies
3. She enjoys watching chaos unfold
4. He's a perfect target for her matchmaking schemes

Seto arranged several of Seina's marriages and considers him an ongoing "project."
