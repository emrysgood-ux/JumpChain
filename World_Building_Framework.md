### Source Universes

| Universe | Canon Priority | Integration Role |
|----------|---------------|------------------|
| **Tenchi Muyo! OVA** | PRIMARY | Main setting, characters, timeline |
| **Sailor Moon (manga)** | SECONDARY | Protagonist origin (Cosmos), Lambda powers |
| **JumpChain mechanics** | TERTIARY | Resurrection framework, abilities |

### Critical Canon Note

This story follows **OVA continuity exclusively**. Tenchi Universe, Tenchi in Tokyo, and other continuities are NOT referenced.

Key OVA-specific facts:
- Tenchi's mother is **Kiyone Masaki** (NOT Achika)
- **Kiyone Makibi** (Galaxy Police) does NOT exist in OVA
- Yosho is ~730 years old (born ~1262 CE, not ~300 CE)
- Ryoko's release date: August 13, 2001 (first day of Obon)

### Real-World Grounding

The Masaki Shrine is modeled on **Tarojinja** shrine, which has exactly 320 steps. This grounds the fantasy in authentic Japanese geography.

| Feature | Real-World Equivalent |
|---------|----------------------|
| 320 steps | Tarojinja shrine |
| Mountain setting | Rural Okayama |
| Rice paddies | Common in region |
| Traditional architecture | 1920s-era construction |
