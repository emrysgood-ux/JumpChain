## NARRATIVE FUNCTION

### Why "Quiet Years"?
- No major plot events
- Character development focus
- Relationship deepening
- Slice-of-life pacing
- Foundation for Year 13 impact

### What These Years Establish
- Tenchi's complete childhood with support
- Sheldon-Yosho bond development
- Family rhythms that will be disrupted
- Normalcy that makes chaos meaningful
