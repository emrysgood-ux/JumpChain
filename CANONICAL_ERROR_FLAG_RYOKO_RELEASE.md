## THE ERROR

**What I wrote:** Ryoko's release framed as inevitable/destined - seals weakening over time, Yosho "preparing" for her emergence, cosmic events converging toward awakening.

**Canon (OVA):** Ryoko's release is an ACCIDENT. Tenchi, as a curious teenager, explores the forbidden cave and pulls out Tenchi-ken (the sword), which breaks the seal. The seal was stable. Yosho was not expecting her emergence. It was human curiosity, not cosmic destiny.
