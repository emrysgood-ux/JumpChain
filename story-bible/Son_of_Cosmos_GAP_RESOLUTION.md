# GAP 1: COMPLETE CELESTIAL PACK GRANT LIST

## 1.1 Grant Philosophy (CONFIRMED)

**Cosmos's Selection Criteria:**
- **ATMOSPHERIC, NOT GAME-LIKE:** No popup windows, stat screens, or level-up notifications
- **INTUITIVE DELIVERY:** Abilities arrive as sudden knowledge or gradual skill improvement
- **PHASE-APPROPRIATE:** All grants present from Day 1 but manifest according to Phase progression
- **CHARACTER-BLIND:** Sheldon does not know these are "grants" — he experiences them as natural talent

**Cosmos's Approach:**
> "Cosmos selected abilities that ENHANCE Sheldon's existing identity while adding nurturing capabilities. She didn't replace the scientist with a caretaker — she made the scientist ALSO an exceptional caretaker."

**Three Grant Categories:**
1. DOMESTIC/NURTURING — Cooking, comfort, caregiving
2. SCIENCE/MAKING — His existing expertise enhanced
3. TEACHING/MENTORING — Bridge between 1 and 2

**What Cosmos DOES NOT Grant:**
- Combat powers (not his domain)
- Destructive abilities (against his nature)
- Mind control (violates autonomy)
- Powers that would overshadow canon characters
- "Power fantasy" abilities
