## Years 2-12 Overview

### Year 2 (April 1990 – March 1991)
- **Tenchi:** Age 5→6, Kindergarten 年長 (nenchō, final year)
- **Theme:** Deepening bonds, village acceptance
- **Historical:** Bubble economy peak/early collapse

### Year 3 (April 1991 – March 1992)
- **Tenchi:** Age 6→7, Elementary 1st Grade begins
- **Theme:** School transition, new social world
- **Historical:** Bubble collapse accelerates, Gulf War aftermath

### Year 4 (April 1992 – March 1993) — REVELATION YEAR
- **Tenchi:** Age 7→8, Elementary 2nd Grade
- **Theme:** "First Blade" — Tenchi's sword training begins (July)
- **KEY EVENT:** Sheldon reveals truth about reincarnation to Yosho

### Year 5 (April 1993 – March 1994)
- **Tenchi:** Age 8→9, Elementary 3rd Grade
- **Theme:** Training deepens, joint practice sessions
- **Historical:** Heisei recession continues

### Year 6 (April 1994 – March 1995)
- **Tenchi:** Age 9→10, Elementary 4th Grade
- **Theme:** Growing competence, independence seeds
- **Historical:** Great Hanshin Earthquake (Jan 1995)

### Year 7 (April 1995 – March 1996)
- **Tenchi:** Age 10→11, Elementary 5th Grade
- **Theme:** Pre-adolescence, complex thinking emerges
- **Historical:** Aum Shinrikyo attacks, post-quake recovery

### Year 8 (April 1996 – March 1997)
- **Tenchi:** Age 11→12, Elementary 6th Grade (final year)
- **Theme:** Elementary graduation, childhood ends
- **KEY EVENT:** Seven-year anniversary of Sheldon's arrival

### Year 9 (April 1997 – March 1998)
- **Tenchi:** Age 12→13, Middle School 1st Grade begins
- **Theme:** Adolescence, normal independence phase
- **Historical:** Asian financial crisis

### Year 10 (April 1998 – March 1999)
- **Tenchi:** Age 13→14, Middle School 2nd Grade
- **Theme:** Teen identity, seal weakening awareness begins
- **Historical:** Economic stagnation

### Year 11 (April 1999 – March 2000)
- **Tenchi:** Age 14→15, Middle School 3rd Grade (final year)
- **Theme:** Middle school graduation, approaching maturity
- **Historical:** Y2K preparations, millennium anxiety

### Year 12 (April 2000 – March 2001)
- **Tenchi:** Age 15→16, High School 1st Grade begins
- **Theme:** Final "normal" year, seal breaks imminent

### Year 13 (April 2001+) — CANON BEGINS
- **Tenchi:** Age 16→17
