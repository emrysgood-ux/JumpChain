## THE RELEASE (Summer 2001)

### What Happens
1. Tenchi (17) steals key from Katsuhito
2. Enters forbidden cave
3. Uses Tenchi-ken to break seal
4. Ryoko awakens
5. Chaos ensues

### Why Now?
- Yosho's 700-year plan reaches fruition
- Tenchi is ready (trained, mature enough)
- Ryoko is rehabilitated (watched family 5+ years)
- Time for next phase

### The Difference (This Story)
**Canon:** Tenchi faces this alone, household forms around crisis
**Found Family:** Tenchi has support network, household EXISTS

## WASHU'S EMERGENCE

### Context
- Washu imprisoned by Kagato 5000+ years
- Freed during Kagato confrontation
- Most intelligent being in universe
- Sheldon's Lambda = FASCINATING anomaly

### Her Investigation of Sheldon
**What She Detects:**
- Unknown cosmic energy signature
- Not Chousin-derived
- "Another god's fingerprints"
- Lambda Power (doesn't know the name)

**Her Conclusion:**
- Sheldon touched by something outside their experiment
- Different dimensional origin
- Cosmos operates beyond Chousin awareness
- "He's not from here. I mean REALLY not from here."

**Her Approach:**
- Scientific curiosity, not threat response
- Studies subtly at first
- Eventually may ask directly
- Respects his privacy (mostly)

## KAGATO CONFRONTATION

### Events (OVA 1 Episode 5-6)
- Kagato arrives seeking Washu
- Confrontation with household

### Found Family Difference

## SHELDON'S ROLE (Year 13)

### What He Does
- Cooks (always)
- Maintains stability
- Provides emotional anchor
- Welcomes newcomers
- Doesn't fight

### What He Doesn't Do
- Engage in combat
- Reveal his nature (yet)
- Compete for attention
- Interfere with Tenchi's destiny

### The Lambda Effect
- His food helps everyone heal
- Infusion works on trauma
- Household feels safe
- Newcomers drawn to kitchen
