## GXP NOVEL TRANSLATIONS

### Complete Resource

**Index URL:** http://tenchimuyotranslationblog.blogspot.com/p/gxp.html

### Volume Count
- **Total Volumes:** 18+ (ongoing as of 2025)
- **Translation Status:** All volumes translated (abridged form, revisions in progress)
- **Translator:** Eff Efferson

### Why GXP Novels Matter

Per canon hierarchy:
- Kajishima was reportedly unhappy with GXP anime director's (Nabeshin) liberties
- Started writing novels as alternative/correction
- **GXP novels trump GXP anime when conflicts exist**
- OVA 4 and 5 follow novel continuity, not anime
- Contains critical expanded information

### Key Information from GXP Novels

| Topic | Content | Relevance |
|-------|---------|-----------|
| Tenchi's Immortality Grant | Tenchi unconsciously grants immortality to harem | Future story reference |
| Seina at Kiyone's Death | Seina present, believes his bad luck killed her | Character backstory |
| Expanded Masaki Family | Details on village members, relationships | Character profiles |
| Galactic Politics | Government structure, laws, treaties | World-building |
