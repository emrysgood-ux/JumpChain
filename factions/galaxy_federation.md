# The Galaxy Federation

## Overview
The Galaxy Federation is the primary political alliance governing the Milky Way Galaxy, co-led by the Juraian Empire and the Seniwa Empire. It provides the framework for interstellar law, commerce, and cooperation.

---

## History

### Before the Federation
- Jurai and Seniwa maintained Cold War-level tensions
- Competition for galactic influence
- Separate spheres of power
- Frequent friction

### Formation
The Federation was created through:
- Mikumo Kuramitsu's diplomatic efforts (Seniwa)
- Transformation of Juraian Imperial Academy into Galaxy Academy
- Pooling of military resources into Galaxy Police
- Treaties establishing shared governance

### Key Figures in Formation
| Figure | Contribution |
|--------|--------------|
| Mikumo Kuramitsu | Opened Seniwa, negotiated alliance |
| Washu | Led Academy independence movement |
| Jurai Emperor (historical) | Agreed to power sharing |

---

## Structure

### Dual Leadership
The Federation is effectively led by two powers:

| Power | Contribution | Dominance Area |
|-------|--------------|----------------|
| Jurai | Royal Tree fleet, political legitimacy | Military supremacy, tradition |
| Seniwa | Galaxy Police, administrative capacity | Law enforcement, bureaucracy |

### Major Institutions

#### Galaxy Police
- Primary law enforcement
- Derived from Kuramitsu family forces
- Protects commerce and trade
- Handles criminal matters

#### Galaxy Academy
- Educational center
- Politically neutral ground
- Trains future leaders
- Research facilities

#### Galaxy Army
- Military operations beyond GP scope
- Joint Jurai-Seniwa command
- Large-scale threats
- Derived from Seniwa forces

---

## Member States

### Core Powers
| State | Status | Specialty |
|-------|--------|-----------|
| Jurai | Dominant | Military, royal trees |
| Seniwa | Dominant | Police, administration |

### Member Worlds
- Numerous planets under Federation banner
- Varying levels of autonomy
- Required to follow Federation law
- Protected by GP and Jurai

### Non-Members
| Entity | Status | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Renza Federation | Independent | Separate alliance |
| Pirate Guilds | Hostile | Criminal organizations |
| Underdeveloped worlds | Excluded | Like Earth (officially) |

---

## Laws & Policies

### Criminal Justice
- Galaxy Police handles enforcement
- Danger level classifications for criminals
- Bounty systems for independent hunters
- Central detention facilities

### Commerce
- Protected trade routes
- Anti-piracy operations
- Standard currency and exchange
- Trade agreements between worlds

### Citizenship
- Federation citizens have rights across space
- Body enhancement available
- Protection under GP jurisdiction
- Travel privileges

### Restrictions
- "Underdeveloped" planets cannot join
- Residents cannot travel intergalactically (usually)
- Exceptions require special circumstances
- Earth is technically in this category

---

## Technology Sharing

### Standard Technology
The Federation provides:
- Body enhancement procedures
- Guardian systems
- Faster-than-light travel
- Communication networks

### Restricted Technology
Some technology remains exclusive:
- Royal Tree ships (Jurai only)
- Highest-tier weapons
- First-generation enhancements
- Cabbit creation (Washu only)

---

## Political Dynamics

### Power Balance
Jurai and Seniwa maintain careful balance:
- Neither can dominate completely
- Institutions serve as neutral ground
- Intermarriage between noble families
- Shared interests in stability

### Noble Houses
Both empires have internal factions:
- Jurai's four houses
- Seniwa's Kuramitsu dominance
- Cross-empire alliances
- Marriage politics

### External Threats
Federation unity strengthens against:
- Pirate guilds
- Renza Federation (historically)
- Cosmic threats (rare)
- Internal rebellion

---

## Relationship with Earth

### Official Status
- Earth is "underdeveloped" by Federation standards
- Not a member world
- Residents cannot normally join GP or travel
- No official first contact

### Exceptions
The Masaki family has special status:
- Juraian heritage through Yosho
- Access to GP recruitment
- Protection from Jurai
- Enhancement available

### Unofficial Situation
In practice:
- Several Earthlings enhanced and integrated
- Masaki household as diplomatic bridge
- Earth protected but not formally
- Potential for future formal relations

---

## Military Capabilities

### Galaxy Police
- Fleet of patrol vessels
- Regional stations
- Rapid response capability
- Criminal investigation

### Galaxy Army
- Larger scale operations
- Heavy combat vessels
- Planetary defense
- Combined Jurai-Seniwa command

### Juraian Royal Fleet
- Supreme military power
- Light Hawk Wing capability
- Deterrent against all threats
- Technically separate but cooperative

---

## Story Significance

The Galaxy Federation represents:
- The political backdrop for galactic society
- How Jurai and Seniwa coexist
- The framework for law and order
- Why Earth characters can access space
- The organization GP characters serve
- The scale of the Tenchi Muyo universe

## For Story Use

### When Referencing the Federation
- It's the main political structure
- GP is its enforcement arm
- Jurai and Seniwa share leadership
- Earth is outside but connected
- Most spacefaring characters are Federation citizens
