# The Kuramitsu Family

## Overview
The Kuramitsu Family is the ruling dynasty of the Seniwa Empire and the dominant force behind the Galaxy Police. For approximately 20,000 years, they have shaped galactic politics, law enforcement, and interstellar relations.

---

## History

### Origins
The Kuramitsu family rose to prominence on Seniwa:
- Ancient noble lineage
- Built power through military capability
- Eventually unified Seniwa under their rule

### The Washu Connection
Approximately 20,000 years ago:
- Prince Mikamo Kuramitsu married Washu Hakubi
- They had a son, Mikumo
- When Mikamo was recalled by his father, he fled with Mikumo
- Seniwa closed its borders after this incident

### Mikumo's Era
Mikumo Kuramitsu transformed Seniwa:
- Gifted with extended life by his mother Washu
- Ruled for over 10,000 years
- Opened Seniwa's borders
- Negotiated the Galaxy Federation alliance with Jurai
- His private military became the Galaxy Police

## Power & Influence

### Galaxy Police
The GP is essentially a Kuramitsu institution:
- "Galaxy Police forces...derived from the personal forces of the Kuramitsu family"
- Family members in key positions
- Institutional loyalty
- Funding and resources

### Political Weight
The family's influence extends to:
- Federation politics
- Military operations
- Economic decisions
- Diplomatic relations

### Balance with Jurai
Despite their power:
- Cannot match Jurai's Royal Trees
- Maintain careful alliance
- Intermarriage with Juraian nobility
- Complementary rather than competitive

## Physical Traits

### Seniwan Characteristics
Kuramitsu family members typically display:
- Dark complexions
- Blue eyes
- Blonde hair
- "Extremely dominant" traits in offspring

These characteristics persist even in mixed marriages.

## Relationship with Washu

### The Hidden Connection
Most don't know:
- Washu was married to Prince Mikamo
- Mikumo is her son
- The family descends from a Choushin goddess
- This explains some unusual traits

### Current Status
- Washu doesn't advertise this history
- Family may not know their true origins
- Mihoshi's impossible luck may relate to this
- Connection to divine is buried
