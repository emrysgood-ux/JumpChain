# The Juraian Empire

## Overview
The Juraian Empire is the dominant political power in the Milky Way Galaxy, ruling through the divine blessing of the goddess Tsunami and the power of the Royal Trees. It is the largest empire in the known galaxy.

---

## Government Structure

### The Imperial House
Four noble houses share power:

#### House Masaki
- **Status**: First among equals, descended from the first Emperor
- **Characteristics**: Favors exploration over rulership
- **Notable Members**: Azusa, Yosho, Tenchi, Tennyo
- **Current Head**: Emperor Azusa Masaki Jurai

#### House Kamiki
- **Status**: Powerful through political maneuvering
- **Characteristics**: Masters of alliance and marriage
- **Notable Members**: Seto, Utsutsumi, Noike
- **Key Figure**: Seto Kamiki Jurai ("Devil Princess")

#### House Amaki
- **Status**: Most ambitious house
- **Characteristics**: Produced majority of historical emperors
- **Notable Members**: Various historical rulers

#### House Tatsuki
- **Status**: Least politically forceful
- **Characteristics**: Largest families, many foreign alliances
- **Notable Members**: Many through marriage connections

### Current Leadership

| First Empress | Funaho | Masaki (by marriage) |
| Second Empress | Misaki | Kamiki |

## Political System

### Succession
Determined by Royal Tree connections:
- Higher-status trees indicate higher ranking
- First-generation partnerships are most prestigious
- Can be complicated by abdication (Yosho)
- Political marriages shift power

### The Four Houses Balance
- Houses compete for influence
- Marriage alliances shift power
- Emperor comes from any house (theoretically)
- Seto Kamiki is master of this system

### Foreign Relations
| Power | Relationship |
|-------|--------------|
| Seniwa | Allied (Galaxy Federation) |
| Galaxy Police | Strong cooperation |
| Renza Federation | Tense but improving |
| Earth | Protected (Masaki family) |

---

## Military Power

### Royal Tree Fleet
The backbone of Juraian military might:
- Tree-ships with Light Hawk Wing capability
- First and second-generation trees lead
- Third-generation trees form bulk of fleet
- No conventional force can challenge them

### Power Scale
| Ship Class | Capability |
|------------|------------|
| Tsunami-fune | Supreme command, 10 LHW |
| First-Gen | Independent LHW generation |
| Second-Gen | Independent LHW generation |
| Third-Gen+ | Combined LHW generation |

### Defense Strategy
- Royal Trees deter most threats
- GP handles police matters
- Pirates remain a persistent issue
- Major threats are rare

## Society & Culture

### Class Structure
1. **Imperial Family** (four houses)
2. **Noble Families** (Royal Tree bonds)
3. **Enhanced Citizens** (body enhancement)
4. **Common Citizens** (standard population)
5. **Non-Juraians** (residents, visitors)

### Enhancement
- Body enhancement is widespread
- Provides superhuman capabilities
- Extends lifespans significantly
- Creates obvious class markers

### Religion
- Tsunami venerated as patron goddess
- Royal Trees considered sacred
- Emperor's legitimacy tied to Tsunami's blessing
- Ceremonies blend political and religious

### Cultural Values
- Honor and duty
- Long-term thinking (centuries-scale)
- Exploration tradition (House Masaki)
- Political cunning (House Kamiki)

## Historical Background

### Founding (150,000 years ago)
- First Emperor discovered Tsunami
- She blessed House Masaki with Royal Trees
- Empire built around this divine gift
- Pirate origins before settlement

### Era of Expansion
- Jurai grew to dominate the galaxy
- Conflicts with Seniwa
- Eventually formed Galaxy Federation
- Achieved current dominance

### Recent History
- Emperor Azusa's long reign
- Yosho's exile to Earth
- Discovery of Tenchi
- Integration with Earth (limited)

## Relationship with Earth

### The Masaki Connection
- Yosho fled to Earth 700 years ago
- Married local woman (Funaho's arrangement)
- Tenchi born from this lineage
- Earth is technically Jurai-protected

### Current Status
- Not publicly known on Earth
- Masaki family has special status
- Earth residents can receive enhancement
- Potential for formal relations unclear
