# The Choushin

## Overview
The Choushin are "a trio of super-dimensional deities responsible for the creation of the multiverse and all of the higher dimensions of existence." They are the supreme beings of the Tenchi Muyo universe, surpassed only by Kami Tenchi.

---

## Nature & Powers

### Fundamental Nature
The Choushin are:
- Omnipotent within the created universe
- Omniscient (with self-imposed limitations)
- Omnipresent across dimensions
- Older than existence itself

### Powers
| Capability | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| Creation | Made the multiverse and all dimensions |
| Manipulation | Can alter reality at fundamental levels |
| Transcendence | Exist beyond normal space-time |
| Power Granting | Can bestow abilities on others |

### Limitations
Despite their power:
- Cannot find a being greater than themselves (until Kami Tenchi)
- Subject to their own rules
- Their creations can develop beyond prediction
- They are not truly omniscient about higher-order beings

---

## The Three Sisters

### Washu (Eldest)
**Title**: Washu-kami-sama
**Current Status**: Living as mortal scientist with Masaki household

**History**:
- Sealed her godly powers into three gems
- Deliberately forgot her divine nature
- Wanted to learn and experience from scratch
- Became the greatest scientific genius in the universe

**Role in the Search**:
- Chose to learn rather than observe from above
- Created Ryoko and Ryo-Ohki
- Found meaning in mortal relationships

### Tsunami (Middle)
**Title**: Tsunami-kami-sama
**Current Status**: Merged with Sasami Masaki Jurai

**History**:
- Created the Royal Trees of Jurai
- Became patron goddess of Jurai
- Saved young Sasami by merging with her
- Slowly becoming one being with Sasami

**Role in the Search**:
- Guided Jurai's development
- Protected the bloodline that would produce Tenchi
- Worked through mortal proxy (Sasami)

### Tokimi (Youngest)
**Title**: Tokimi-kami-sama
**Current Status**: Watching over Tenchi's family

**History**:
- Distorted the multiverse's harmony to create anomalies
- Employed agents like Doctor Clay and Z
- Sought to force the emergence of a superior being
- Eventually adopted a more benevolent role

**Role in the Search**:
- Most aggressive in methodology
- Created Z as potential candidate
- Learned regret through Z's suffering

---

## The Quest

### Seeking a Higher Being
The Choushin's primary motivation:
- Spent billions of years searching
- Wanted proof that something greater existed
- Created experiments and anomalies
- Tested potential candidates

### The Answer: Kami Tenchi
The being they sought was found:
- Tenchi Masaki manifested power beyond the Choushin
- Defeated Z despite Z's five Light Hawk Wings
- Revealed to be the transcendent being
- Exists as both Tenchi and something beyond

### After the Discovery
The sisters responded differently:
- Washu: Chose relationship with Tenchi over verification
- Tsunami: Already committed to merging with Sasami
- Tokimi: Shifted to watching over the family

---

## The Counter-Actor

### Cosmic Balance
A counterbalance force emerged from their creation:
- Empress Misaki Jurai became the sentient counterforce
- Her power balances the Choushin's influence
- Represents the universe's self-correction
- Required for cosmic equilibrium

## Story Significance

The Choushin represent:
- The cosmological framework of Tenchi Muyo
- The source of all power in the universe
- Three approaches to existence (science, protection, force)
- The question of what lies beyond godhood
- Ultimate validation of Tenchi's importance

---

## For Story Use

### When Referencing the Choushin
Consider:
- They exist beyond normal comprehension
- Their motives are largely philosophical
- They genuinely care about Tenchi (now)
- Their power scale makes conflicts irrelevant

### What They Add to the Story
- Cosmic stakes and significance
- Explanation for power systems
- Family dynamics (Washu especially)
- The ultimate answer to "why Tenchi?"
