Main Series & OVAs
Kagato: A major recurring villain, an ancient space pirate who caused much of the turmoil before the series started.
Doll (Mexiah Furan): An artificial being from the Senshi Civilization, a primary antagonist in Tenchi Muyo! War on Geminar.
Z: The main antagonist of the third OVA, a powerful being manipulated by Tokimi who seeks to destroy reality.
Seto Kamiki Jurai: Tenchi's ancestor, often a chaotic force, acting as a manipulator and sometimes antagonist in GXP.
Misaki Masaki Jurai: Tenchi's ancestor and a powerful figure who causes problems, especially in the OVAs.
Misao Kuramitsu: A Galaxy Police officer and antagonist in the third OVA. 
Movies
Kain: A powerful criminal and main villain in Tenchi Muyo! in Love.
Haruna: A former lover of Yosho, trapping Tenchi in an alternate dimension in Tenchi Forever!. 
Tenchi in Tokyo
Yugi: A warped antagonist who tries to conquer Earth.
Hotsuma, Tsugaru, Matori: Servants of Yugi in Tenchi in Tokyo. 
Manga/Other
Dark Washu: A malevolent version of Washu.
The Shima Brothers: Antagonists in the manga.
