# Starships of the Tenchi Muyo Universe

## Overview
The Tenchi Muyo universe features a diverse array of spacecraft, from living Royal Tree ships to advanced Galaxy Police vessels and everything in between.

---

## Royal Tree Ships (Jurai)

Royal Tree ships are biological vessels with a sentient tree at their core, drawing power from a subspace network connected to Tsunami-no-ki.

### Tsunami-fune
- **Generation**: 0 (Tsunami herself)
- **Partner**: Sasami/Tsunami
- **Capabilities**: 10 Light Hawk Wings (maximum possible)
- **Status**: Active
- **Notes**: The most powerful ship in existence; can control all Jurai ships

### Mikagami ("Water Reflection")
- **Generation**: 2nd
- **Partner**: Seto Kamiki Jurai / Kuis Panta
- **Capabilities**: Triple-Z attack ("Genocidal Dance"), Light Hawk Wings
- **Status**: Active
- **Notes**: Causes emotional distress in combat; required creation of Kuis Panta after Seto's coma

### Ryu-Oh
- **Generation**: 1st
- **Partner**: Ayeka Masaki Jurai
- **Capabilities**: Standard 1st generation powers
- **Status**: Destroyed by Sōja, regenerating
- **Notes**: Destroyed protecting Tenchi; seed preserved for regeneration

### Funaho
- **Generation**: 1st
- **Partner**: Yosho/Katsuhito
- **Capabilities**: Standard 1st generation powers
- **Status**: Dormant on Earth
- **Notes**: Hidden near Masaki Shrine; named after Yosho's mother

### Mizuki
- **Generation**: Unknown
- **Partner**: Kiriko Masaki
- **Capabilities**: Compatible with Fuku; can combine with Kamidake II
- **Status**: Active
- **Notes**: Introduced to replace problematic Mikagami-Fuku pairing

## Cabbit Ships

Cabbit ships are unique biological vessels created by Washu, combining animal and mineral lifeforms.

### Ryo-Ohki
- **Type**: Cabbit-class
- **Creator**: Washu
- **Partner**: Ryoko Hakubi
- **Capabilities**:
  - With three gems: 10 Light Hawk Wings
  - Without gems: Equal to 3rd generation Jurai ship
- **Forms**: Cabbit, child humanoid, adult humanoid, spaceship
- **Status**: Active
- **Notes**: Originally controlled by Kagato; destroyed 28 planets

### Ken-Ohki
- **Type**: Cabbit-class
- **Partner**: Nagi (bounty hunter)
- **Capabilities**: Similar to Ryo-Ohki
- **Status**: Active (Tenchi Universe continuity)
- **Notes**: Has romantic feelings for Ryo-Ohki

### Fuku
- **Type**: Cabbit-class
- **Creator**: Washu
- **Partner**: Seina Yamada
- **Capabilities**:
  - Self-evolution superior to Ryo-Ohki
  - Functions without gems
  - Merges with Juraian treeship technology
- **Status**: Active
- **Notes**: Core contains soul of executed criminal "Widow" (memories erased)

## Galaxy Police Vessels

### Kamidake (Original)
- **Type**: GP Cruiser
- **Commander**: Seina Yamada
- **Status**: Destroyed/Replaced
- **Notes**: Seina's first command

### Kamidake II
- **Type**: Advanced GP Vessel / Hybrid
- **Designer**: Washu
- **Commander**: Seina Yamada
- **Power Source**: Fuku
- **Capabilities**:
  - Self-upgrading and adapting
  - Can generate Light Hawk Wings (when combined with Mikagami and Mizuki)

- **Notes**: Most advanced GP vessel ever constructed

- **Notes**: Featured prominently in Tenchi Universe

### Daidalos

- **Status**: Active

### Yomotsuookami

## Ship Classifications

| Tier | Examples | Capabilities |
|------|----------|--------------|
| God-Tier | Tsunami-fune | 10 Light Hawk Wings, universal control |
| Supreme | Ryo-Ohki (with gems), Kamidake II (combined) | 10 Light Hawk Wings |
