# Planet Jurai

## Overview
Jurai is the capital planet of the galaxy's largest empire, located in the Milky Way Galaxy. The name combines the kanji for "tree" (樹) and "thunder" (雷), reflecting its deep connection to the Royal Trees.

## Historical Background

### Discovery of Tsunami (150,000 years ago)
- A man discovered the goddess Tsunami sleeping on Jurai
- Tsunami detected "potential" in this man and theorized his descendants would produce a superior being
- She pledged to protect House Masaki and blessed them with Royal Trees
- This man became the first Emperor of Jurai

### Pirate Origins
- Juraian traditions are still based on their pirating past before settling the planet
- Citizens include descendants of various pirate factions
- Some groups maintain independent pirate guilds outside imperial control

## Government Structure

### The Imperial House
The empire is governed by four noble houses:

#### House Masaki
- **Status**: First among equals, descended from the first Emperor
- **Characteristics**: Historically produces fewer emperors; members favor exploration over rulership
- **Notable Members**: Emperor Azusa, Prince Yosho, Tenchi Masaki, Tennyo Masaki

#### House Kamiki
- **Status**: Gained political prominence through Seto's influence and strategic marriages
- **Characteristics**: Masters of political maneuvering and alliances
- **Notable Members**: Seto Kamiki Jurai, Utsutsumi Kamiki, Noike Kamiki Jurai

#### House Amaki
- **Status**: Most ambitious of the four houses
- **Characteristics**: Has produced the majority of emperors historically
- **Notable Members**: Various emperors throughout history

#### House Tatsuki
- **Status**: Least forceful politically
- **Characteristics**: Produces most Juraian royalty through large families and foreign alliances
- **Notable Members**: Connected to many noble marriages

### Current Leadership
- **Emperor**: Azusa Masaki Jurai
- **First Empress**: Funaho (Earthling origin)
- **Second Empress**: Misaki (House Kamiki)
- **First Heir**: Prince Yosho (abdicated, lives on Earth as Katsuhito)
- **Second Heir**: Tenchi Masaki

## Royal Trees & Succession

### Nature of Royal Trees
- Sentient descendants of Tsunami's power
- Bond with individuals and serve as the heart of starships
- Provide power, extended lifespans, and enhanced abilities to bonded royals
- All tree-ships draw power from a subspace network connected to Tsunami-no-ki

### Tree Generations
- **First Generation**: Direct seeds from Tsunami-no-ki (rarest, most powerful)
- **Second Generation**: Seeds from first-generation trees (still extremely powerful)
- **Third Generation+**: Descendants of second-generation trees (require combination to generate Light Hawk Wings)

### Succession Rules
- Inheritance depends on Royal Tree connections
- Higher-status trees indicate higher succession ranking
- Bonding with a first-generation tree seed grants immediate high status

---

## Juraian Capabilities

### Body Enhancement
- Juraians possess "unsurpassed" mastery of biological enhancement
- Regular citizens demonstrate superhuman strength
- Can generate force fields and energy discharges
- Standard procedures can extend non-Juraian lifespans to approximately 2,000 years

### Guardian Systems
- Energy-based suits providing armor and weaponry
- Azaka and Kamidake are examples of Guardian units serving the princesses
- Combine biological enhancement with technological augmentation

### Lifespan
- Enhanced Juraians live thousands of years
- Emperor Azusa is over 5,000 years old
- Prince Yosho is over 700 years old
- Royal Tree bonding extends life indefinitely with proper maintenance

## Key Locations on Jurai

### The Royal Palace
- Seat of imperial power
- Houses Tsunami-no-ki (the original tree) at its center
- Connected to Tenju, the palace tree form of Tsunami

### The Royal Arboretum
- Where Royal Trees are cultivated
- Bonding ceremonies take place here
- Sacred ground with restricted access

### The Imperial Academy (Original)
- Now known as Galaxy Academy
- Originally the Juraian Imperial Academy
- Made independent after political tensions with Seniwa

## Culture & Society

### Writing System
- Uses a rune-based writing system resembling Norse/Germanic script
- Serves as intergalactic lingua franca in many regions

### Class Structure
- Imperial Family (four houses)
- Noble families with Royal Tree bonds
- Common citizens with enhancement access
- Non-enhanced population

### Religious Significance
- Tsunami is venerated as patron goddess
- Royal Trees are considered sacred
- The empire's legitimacy derives from Tsunami's blessing

---

## Relationships with Other Powers

### Galaxy Federation
- Jurai is the dominant power in the Federation
- Shares influence with Seniwa
- Controls much of galactic trade and policy

### Seniwa
- Historic rival, now ally through Galaxy Federation
- Previous Cold War-level tensions before Federation formation
- Kuramitsu family rules Seniwa

### Renza Federation
- Independent alliance separate from Galaxy Federation
- Different core beliefs and values
- Some worlds rely on magic rather than science

## Story Significance

Jurai serves as the cosmic backdrop for much of the Tenchi Muyo universe, representing:
- The source of supernatural power through Royal Trees
- Political intrigue and succession drama
- The connection between Earth and the wider galaxy
- The origin point for many characters' journeys
