## PART III: ROUTES & JOURNEYS

### ROUTE A: SHRINE ↔ VILLAGE (Daily Path)

**The 320 Steps:**

| Segment | Steps | Experience |
|---------|-------|------------|
| Base to Torii | Path (~20m) | Forest closing in; anticipation |
| Lower Third (1-100) | 100 steps | Steady rhythm; warming up |
| Middle Third (101-220) | 120 steps | Working hard; breathing deeper |
| Upper Third (221-320) | 100 steps | Final push; shrine appearing |
| Courtyard Arrival | — | Open space; Funaho tree |

**Going Down (easier but still significant):**
- Tenchi often runs ahead
- Must watch footing
- Faster but requires attention
- Different views looking down

**Continuation to Village:**
From bus stop base, continue ~0.3 km to village center on paved road.

**Time Budget:**
| Direction | Adult | With Tenchi |
|-----------|-------|-------------|
| Up | 15 min | 20-25 min (carries Tenchi part way) |
| Down | 10 min | 15 min (Tenchi slower on steps) |

---

### ROUTE B: SHRINE ↔ TSUKINO HOUSE

**Path Description:**
Mountain path through forest; not the main road.

| Segment | Distance | Time | Notes |
|---------|----------|------|-------|
| Shrine to fork | 0.3 km | 5 min | Forest path |
| Fork to house | 0.7 km | 10 min | Narrower; some uphill |

**Alternative (Road):**
Via village road, ~2 km, 25 min walk. Used when carrying supplies.

### ROUTE C: VILLAGE CENTER CIRCUIT

**Sheldon's Shopping Route:**

```
Start: Enter village from shrine path
  ↓
1. Ito General Store (supplies)
  ↓
2. Yamazaki Grocery (fresh food)
  ↓
3. Tanaka Hardware (if repairs needed)
  ↓
4. Post Office (mail, banking)
  ↓
Return: Same path back
```

**Efficient Shopping (30-45 min):**
- Know what you need
- Move purposefully
- Brief greetings, not long chats
- Leave before fatigue

**Social Shopping (1-2 hours):**
- Chat with Ito-san
- Browse
- Tea offered at shop
- Community building

---

### ROUTE D: TO KURASHIKI

**Journey Steps:**

1. Walk from shrine to station (25 min)
   - Down steps
   - Through village
   - To station

2. Wait for train (variable, 0-30 min)

3. Train to Kurashiki (15 min)
   - Pleasant ride
   - Rice paddies, small towns
   - Views of hills

4. Kurashiki Station area
   - Exit to shopping
   - Or walk to Bikan Quarter (15 min)

**Total Time:** 1-1.5 hours one way
**Cost:** ¥640 round trip

### ROUTE E: TENCHI'S SCHOOL BUS ROUTE

**Morning:**
1. Shrine → Bus stop (down 320 steps, ~10 min)
2. Wait at Jinja-mae stop (~5 min)
3. Bus arrives 8:30 AM precisely
4. Bus route through villages
5. Arrive kindergarten ~8:45 AM

**Afternoon:**
1. Bus departs kindergarten ~2:00 PM
2. Arrives Jinja-mae ~2:15 PM
3. Adult waiting (Katsuhito or Sheldon)
4. Walk up steps to shrine

**Bus Details:**
- Line 47 (Kamogata-Yama Line)
- Driver: Nakamura Kenji (knows all regulars)
- Other children board at various stops
- Yellow kindergarten hats visible
