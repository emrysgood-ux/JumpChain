- Yosho's partner; permanently rooted 700 years
- Contains power to generate Light Hawk Wings
- Connected to Tsunami
- Source of Yosho's extended lifespan (within range)

**Effect on Sensitive Visitors:**

- Lambda resonance (unconscious recognition)
- Hikaru (cat) comfortable near it (unlike cave)

The Ito couple's general store is the central node of village information flow.

**Information Sources:**
1. **Ito Haruki** — Village historian; curious; loves to talk; non-judgmental
2. **Ito Fumiko** — Controls gossip flow; more reserved; if she accepts you, village women accept you
3. **Bus Driver Nakamura** — Sees all passengers; notes new faces
4. **Postmaster** — Knows who corresponds with whom
5. **Shopkeepers** — Observe purchasing patterns

**What Gets Noticed:**
- Who bought what (and implications)
- Who walked with whom
- Who looks tired/happy/worried
- Strangers immediately noticed and remembered
- Absence from usual patterns

**Integration Markers:**
- Being greeted by name
- Offered tea at shops
- Included in casual conversation
- Asked for opinions
- Invited to help with events
