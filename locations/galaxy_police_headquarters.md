# Galaxy Police Headquarters & Facilities

## Overview
The Galaxy Police (GP) is the primary intergalactic law enforcement agency operating across the Galaxy Federation. Its infrastructure spans multiple systems, with facilities ranging from the main headquarters to regional stations and the prestigious GP Academy.

---

## Organization Structure

### Leadership Hierarchy
| Position | Current Holder | Notes |
|----------|---------------|-------|
| Head of Galaxy Police | Mikami Kuramitsu | Also directs GP Academy |
| Head of Docking Maintenance | Minami Kuramitsu | Former Head of GP |
| Various Directors | Multiple | Regional and departmental |

### Kuramitsu Family Dominance
- The Seniwa Empire and Kuramitsu Family dominate GP leadership
- GP forces are derived from the personal forces of the Kuramitsu family
- This grants them significant influence over Federation operations

---

## Primary Functions

### Law Enforcement
- Giving criminals danger levels and classifications
- Capturing dangerous criminals
- Investigating crimes across the galaxy

### Commerce Protection
- Protecting merchant vessels from space pirates
- Safeguarding trade routes
- Anti-piracy operations

### Political Stability
- Supporting Galaxy Federation interests
- Maintaining peace between member worlds
- Emergency response to threats

---

## Major Facilities

### GP Headquarters
- **Location**: Seniwan space (primary)
- **Function**: Central command and administration
- **Features**:
  - Command center for galaxy-wide operations
  - High-security detention facilities
  - Advanced communications hub
  - Strategic planning divisions

### GP Academy
- **Location**: Within Galaxy Academy complex
- **Function**: Training future GP officers
- **Leadership**: Mikami Kuramitsu (Director)
- **Features**:
  - Combat training facilities
  - Ship operation simulators
  - Criminal investigation courses
  - Physical enhancement procedures

### Regional Stations
- Distributed across Galaxy Federation space
- Local law enforcement coordination
- First responder deployment
- Criminal processing centers

### Docking & Maintenance Facilities
- **Head**: Minami Kuramitsu
- Ship repair and upgrade stations
- Supply depots
- Fleet coordination

---

## Technology & Equipment

### Body Enhancement
- Standard procedure for all GP officers
- Extends lifespans to approximately 2,000 years
- Boosts strength and endurance significantly
- Derived from Juraian enhancement technology

### Guardian Systems
- Energy-based suits providing armor and weaponry
- Deployable in combat situations
- Various configurations available

### Control Cube
- Multi-functional device issued to all officers
- Equipment access and deployment
- Communication and tracking
- Data storage and retrieval

### Starships
- Fleet of patrol vessels
- Advanced cruisers for special operations
- Experimental designs capable of self-evolution (e.g., Kamidake II)

## Policies & Regulations

### Recruitment Restrictions
- Individuals from underdeveloped planets cannot join GP
- Cannot travel intergalactically without special permission
- Exceptions exist for those with Jurai heritage (e.g., Masaki family)

### Criminal Classification
- Danger level assessment for all known criminals
- Priority targeting for high-level threats
- Bounty systems for independent hunters

### Jurisdictional Limits
- Operates within Galaxy Federation space
- Limited authority in Renza Federation
- Coordination with Jurai military for major threats

---

## Training Programs

### Academy Curriculum
1. **Basic Training**
   - Physical conditioning
   - Combat fundamentals
   - Ship operations

2. **Specialization Tracks**
   - Investigation
   - Combat operations
   - Technical/Engineering
   - Command track

3. **Enhancement Procedures**
   - Body enhancement surgery
   - Guardian system training
   - Extended lifespan preparation

### Graduation Requirements
- Minimum competency in all basic areas
- Specialization certification
- Field training completion
- Physical enhancement acceptance

## Story Significance

The Galaxy Police represents:
- Law and order in the galaxy
- Connection between Earth characters and wider universe
- Source of many recurring characters (Mihoshi, Kiyone, Seina, etc.)
- The secular counterpart to Jurai's imperial power
- Career path for Earthlings with proper heritage
