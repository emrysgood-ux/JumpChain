# Galaxy Academy

## Overview
The Galaxy Academy is an enormous educational institution spanning the size of an entire solar system. It serves as the premier center of learning in the galaxy, training students from junior high through collegiate levels across multiple disciplines.

---

## Physical Scale
- Size: Approximately one solar system in extent
- Multiple campuses and facilities
- Housing for students from across the galaxy
- Research facilities, training grounds, and administrative centers

---

## History

### Origins as Juraian Imperial Academy
- Originally established as the "Juraian Imperial Academy" or "Jurai Royal Academy"
- Operated under direct Juraian control
- Served primarily Juraian nobility and allies

### Independence Movement
- Political tensions between Jurai and Seniwa prompted transformation
- Washu led the movement for institutional independence
- Successfully made the Academy independent from either empire
- Restructuring preceded the formation of the Galaxy Federation itself

### Modern Era
- Serves students from all Federation worlds
- Maintains political neutrality
- Governed by academic rather than imperial interests

---

## Leadership

### Current Chairwoman: Airi Masaki
- Wife of Yosho/Katsuhito
- Mother of Kiyone Masaki
- In charge of the entire institution
- Balances academic duties with family connections to Earth

---

## Departments & Divisions

### Galaxy Police Academy
- Dedicated department for training future GP officers
- Located within the larger institution
- Provides specialized law enforcement training
- Graduates become full GP officers upon completion

### Philosophy Department
- Where Washu and Doctor Clay once worked
- Historically prestigious
- Clay and Washu competed for directorship

### Science & Research
- Advanced research facilities
- Washu maintained labs here before her imprisonment
- Cutting-edge galactic technology development

### General Education
- Standard academic curricula
- Multiple grade levels from junior high through university
- Diverse student population
