# The Renza Federation

## Overview
The Renza Federation is an alliance of galactic nations operating independently from the larger Galactic Federation of Jurai and Seniwa. It represents a fundamentally different approach to civilization, with some worlds relying on magic rather than science.

---

## Political Status

### Independence
- Completely separate from the Jurai-Seniwa Galaxy Federation
- Maintains its own government and military
- Different core beliefs and values from the major powers

### Relationship with Galaxy Federation
- Tense but not openly hostile
- Trade relationships exist
- Significant pirate activity complicates relations
- Recent rapprochement through marriages to Seina Yamada

---

## The Five Kingdoms

The Renza Federation is governed by five distinct kingdoms:

### Kihaku Kingdom (Amber Cavalry)
- **Leadership**: Princess Hakuren (1st Princess)
- **Symbol**: Amber/Yellow coloring
- **Specialty**: Cavalry and mounted combat
- **Notable Members**: Hakuren (one of Seina's wives)

### Kasen Kingdom (Fire Spring)
- **Leadership**: King Hakuten Mashiro Kasen
- **Symbol**: Fire/Red coloring
- **Specialty**: Heat and energy-based abilities
- **Culture**: Warrior traditions

### Suire Kingdom (Green Spirit)
- **Leadership**: King Suiten Hyoua Suire
- **Symbol**: Green coloring
- **Specialty**: Nature and spirit connections
- **Notable Members**: Suiren (one of Seina's wives)

### Masui Kingdom (Polished Water)
- **Leadership**: Queen Gyokuren
- **Symbol**: Water/Blue coloring
- **Specialty**: Water-based powers and navigation
- **Notable Members**: Gyokuren (one of Seina's wives)

### Kaou Kingdom (Sakura Flower)
- **Leadership**: King Ranga Baruwemuru Souka
- **Symbol**: Pink/Sakura coloring
- **Population**: Elves
- **Specialty**: Magic and nature affinity
- **Notable Members**: Elven nobility

---

## Unique Characteristics

### Magic vs. Science
- Some Renza worlds operate on magical principles
- "Science does not work on these planets"
- Technology must be adapted or abandoned
- Creates fundamental cultural differences from Galaxy Federation

### The Renza Knights
- Elite warriors of the Federation
- Descended from ancient mecha discoveries
- Thirty thousand years ago, five ancient mecha with Royal Tree Seeds were discovered on planet Terra
- These mecha form the basis for the Federation's military might

---

## Key Locations

### Planet Terra
- Historical discovery site of the five ancient mecha
- Contains Royal Tree Seeds
- Foundation of Renza Knights' power

### The Five Kingdom Capitals
- Each kingdom maintains its own capital world
- Distinct cultures and architectures
- United under Federation banner while maintaining autonomy

## Military Capabilities

### The Ancient Mecha
- Five original mecha from 30,000 years ago
- Powered by Royal Tree Seeds
- Form the backbone of Renza military power
- One was discovered and bonded to Seina Yamada

### Conventional Forces
- Fleet of warships
- Ground forces from each kingdom
- Magic-users from appropriate worlds

### Pirate Connections
- Significant pirate activity within Federation space
- Some kingdoms have historical pirate connections
- Creates tension with Galaxy Police

---

## Relationship with Seina Yamada

### Discovery of the Idol
- Seina discovered a billion-year-old mecha (the "Idol")
- This mecha contained a first-generation Royal Tree Seed
- Bonding with it made Seina third in line to Jurai throne

### Political Marriages
- Four Renza agents (Hakuren, Gyokuren, Karen, Suiren) married Seina
- Created unprecedented political connection between Renza and Galaxy Federation
- Potentially bridges the gap between the two powers

---

## Story Significance

The Renza Federation represents:
- An alternative to the Jurai-Seniwa dominated galaxy
- The existence of magic alongside science in the Tenchi universe
- Political complexity beyond simple good vs. evil
- Source of several of Seina's wives
- Ancient connections to Royal Tree technology
- A wildcard in galactic politics
