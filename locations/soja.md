# The Sōja

## Overview
The Sōja is a massive intergalactic spaceship originally created by Washu. It served as Kagato's base of operations, mobile fortress, and prison for over 5,000 years until its destruction during the events of Tenchi Muyo! OVA.

---

## Name Origin
The ship's name references the real Japanese city of Sōja, located in Okayama Prefecture (established 1954, population approximately 56,755).

---

## Design & Architecture

### Scale
- Massive vessel dwarfing conventional starships
- Contains multiple internal environments
- Houses an alternate dimension within its structure

### Key Features
- **Alternate Universe Prison**: Washu was imprisoned in a pocket dimension within the ship
- **Multiple Deck Configurations**: Various zones for different purposes
- **Advanced Weapons Systems**: Capable of destroying Royal Tree ships
- **Subspace Capabilities**: Can traverse dimensions

### Aesthetics
- Dark, imposing architecture
- Reflects Kagato's personality
- Combines Washu's scientific genius with Kagato's malevolent purpose

---

## History

### Creation
- Designed and built by Washu Hakubi
- Originally intended for research and exploration
- Represented pinnacle of Washu's engineering before imprisonment

### Kagato's Takeover
- Kagato, Washu's former assistant, seized control
- Captured and imprisoned Washu in the ship's alternate universe
- Enslaved Ryoko and Ryo-Ohki using the ship as base
- Used it for 5,000 years of piracy and "Ruins Busting"

### The Terror Campaign
- Under Kagato's control, Ryoko and Ryo-Ohki destroyed 28 planets
- Kagato used the Sōja to search for artifacts
- Destroyed 3,326 archaeological sites seeking ultimate power
- The ship became synonymous with cosmic terror

---

## Capabilities

### Offensive Systems
- Capable of destroying Juraian Royal Tree ships
- Destroyed Ryu-Oh with Tenchi aboard
- Energy weapons of immense power
- Unknown full arsenal (never fully deployed before destruction)

### Defensive Systems
- Shields capable of withstanding conventional attacks
- Subspace evasion capabilities
- Near-impenetrable hull

### Special Features
- Pocket dimension containing Washu's prison/lab
- Navigation through ruins and debris fields
- Long-term life support for extended missions
- Self-sufficient operations

---

## Key Events

### "Kagato Attacks!" (OVA Episode)
1. Kagato kidnapped Ryoko, bringing her to the Sōja
2. Mihoshi accidentally infiltrated the vessel
3. Mihoshi successfully freed Washu from her prison
4. Tenchi confronted Kagato aboard the ship

### The Final Battle
- Tenchi manifested Light Hawk Wings for the first time
- The power surge from divine intervention (after Tenchi's death and resurrection)
- Tenchi bisected both Kagato and the Sōja
- The ship was destroyed, ending its reign of terror

## Layout (Known Areas)

### Command Bridge
- Kagato's throne room
- Ship control systems
- Where final battle occurred

### Prison Dimension
- Washu's containment area
- Actually a functional laboratory (Washu made the best of it)
- Accessed through dimensional gateway

### Hangar Bays
- Where Ryo-Ohki docked
- Assault craft storage
- Entry point for Mihoshi's infiltration

### Corridors & Living Quarters
- Massive internal structure
- Multiple levels
- Trap-laden security zones

## Technological Significance

### Washu's Engineering
- Represents some of Washu's greatest work
- Subspace technology
- Dimensional manipulation
- Power generation systems

### Lost Technology
- With the ship's destruction, much technology was lost
- Some systems may have been unique prototypes
- Washu presumably retained the knowledge to rebuild

## Story Significance

The Sōja represents:
- Kagato's power and ambition given physical form
- The corruption of Washu's genius for evil purposes
- The prison that held the universe's greatest scientist
- The site of Tenchi's awakening to his true power
- A symbol of 5,000 years of terror finally ended
