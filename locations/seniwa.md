# Planet Seniwa

## Overview
Seniwa is the second most influential planet in the galaxy, rivaling Jurai in power and importance. It serves as the home base of the Kuramitsu family and the birthplace of the Galaxy Police.

---

## Physical Characteristics

### Climate
- The planet is covered in snow and ice for much of the year
- Harsh winters dominate the climate
- Cities are built to withstand extreme cold

### Population Traits
- Seniwans typically display dark complexions
- Blue eyes are common
- Blonde hair is the norm
- These traits are "extremely dominant" even in interplanetary marriages

---

## Government & Politics

### The Kuramitsu Dynasty
- Has governed Seniwa for approximately 20,000 years
- Currently led by descendants of Mikumo Kuramitsu
- The family's private military became the core of the Galaxy Police

### Key Historical Figures

#### Prince Mikamo Kuramitsu
- Married Washu Hakubi approximately 20,000 years ago
- Produced heir Mikumo
- When recalled by his father, stole away with his son
- Led to Seniwa closing its borders

#### Mikumo Kuramitsu
- Son of Mikamo and Washu
- Gifted with extended life by his mother
- Ruled for over 10,000 years
- Modernized Seniwa by opening borders
- Established the Galaxy Federation alliance with Jurai

---

## Relationship with Galaxy Police

### Historical Development
- Mikumo's private military evolved into the core of the Galaxy Police
- The Galaxy Army also derives from Seniwan forces
- Seniwa and the Kuramitsu family dominate GP leadership

### Current Influence
- Head of GP traditionally comes from Kuramitsu family
- Mikami Kuramitsu currently heads the GP
- Minami Kuramitsu serves as Head of Docking Maintenance

## Role in Galactic Politics

### Galaxy Federation
- Co-founded with Jurai after centuries of tension
- Shares power with Juraian imperial family
- Provides military and police infrastructure

### Historical Tensions with Jurai
- Before Galaxy Federation formation, maintained Cold War-level relations
- Competition for galactic influence
- Resolved through diplomatic marriage and alliance

### Independence of Galaxy Academy
- Originally the Juraian Imperial Academy
- Washu led movement to make it independent
- Restructuring preceded Galaxy Federation formation

---

## Notable Locations

### Kuramitsu Estate
- Ancestral home of the ruling family
- Center of Seniwan political power

### GP Academy Campus
- Major training facility for Galaxy Police
- Located within Seniwan territory
- Part of the larger Galaxy Academy system

## Technology & Capabilities

### Body Enhancement
- Standard GP procedure extending lifespans to ~2,000 years
- Boosts strength and endurance
- Derived from Juraian technology sharing

### Military Technology
- Advanced starships
- Control Cubes for equipment access
- Guardian Systems

---

## Story Significance

Seniwa represents:
- The secular/military counterpart to Jurai's mystical power
- Origin of law enforcement in the galaxy
- Connection to Washu's past through the Kuramitsu bloodline
- Mihoshi's heritage and her family's galactic influence
