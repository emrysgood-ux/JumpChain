### COMIKET (Comic Market)

**Location:** Tokyo Big Sight (after 1996) / Harumi, Tokyo (1989)
**Event:** Twice yearly — Summer (August), Winter (December)

**1989 STATUS — CRITICAL CONTEXT:**

**C36 (Summer 1989):**
- Approximately 10,000 circles (exhibitors)
- Estimates of ~200,000-300,000 attendees

**⚠️ SUMMER 1989 CRISIS:**
Tsutomu Miyazaki arrested August 1989 (serial child killer found to be otaku)

**Media Backlash:**
- Massive negative coverage of otaku culture
- Famous quote: "There are a million Miyazakis here"
- Attendees harassed by media at Comiket
- Public perception of fans plummeted

**Despite this:** Comiket continued growing
- C37 (Winter 1989): Continued operation
- By 1990: 13,000 circles, 250,000 attendees

**Story Potential:**
- Cultural touchstone for 1989 Japan
- Media panic arc if relevant
- Nobuyuki possibly attends (architecture circles? anime fan?)
- Contrast: wholesome hobby vs. media vilification

**⚠️ SENSITIVITY:** The Miyazaki panic was real and traumatic for Japanese fan communities. Handle with care if referenced.

## VACATION DESTINATIONS

### ONSEN TOWNS (Hot Spring Resorts)

**Kinosaki Onsen (Hyogo Prefecture)**
- 3 hours from Okayama
- Seven public bathhouses
- Yukata strolling tradition
- Crab cuisine (winter)

**Yufuin (Oita Prefecture, Kyushu)**  
- 4+ hours from Okayama
- Artistic, boutique atmosphere
- Mountain backdrop
- Less crowded than Beppu

**Dōgo Onsen (Matsuyama, Shikoku)**
- 3 hours from Okayama (via bridge)
- One of Japan's oldest hot springs
- Historic bathhouse (Spirited Away inspiration, but film is 2001)
- Connected to Natsume Soseki's "Botchan"

**Arima Onsen (Hyogo Prefecture)**
- 2 hours from Okayama
- Near Kobe/Osaka
- Gold and silver springs (different mineral content)
- Historic (mentioned in ancient texts)

**Onsen Etiquette (Story Reference):**
1. Wash thoroughly before entering bath
2. No swimsuits
3. Keep towel out of water
4. No splashing, quiet atmosphere
5. Tattoos may cause issues (yakuza association)

**⚠️ SHELDON NOTE:** As American, onsen visit could be uncomfortable initially. Cultural adjustment scene potential.

### HOKKAIDO

**Travel from Okayama:** Significant (flight or long train journey)

**Summer Destinations:**
- Furano/Biei (lavender fields, rolling hills)

## SCHOOL TRIP STANDARDS (Shūgakuryokō)

Japanese schools have standardized educational trips:

| School Level | Typical Duration | Common Destinations |
|--------------|------------------|---------------------|
| Elementary | 1-2 nights | Regional (local prefectures) |
| Middle School | 2-3 nights | Kyoto/Nara (standard), Hiroshima |
| High School | 3-5 nights | Tokyo, Hokkaido, Okinawa, or abroad |

**Structure:**
- Group activities, strict scheduling
- Educational focus (history, culture)
- Omiyage shopping time allocated
- Group photos at landmarks

**Story Potential:**
- Tenchi's school trips as he ages
- Sheldon potentially chaperoning (if teaching role)
- Different experience from family trips
