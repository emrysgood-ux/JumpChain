**Type:** Character Development

Rest meant sitting with his eyes closed while sounds of meal preparation surrounded him. The clink of bowls. Water running. The scrape of a knife against a cutting board.

Sheldon hadn't cooked a meal in years. His former life had run on convenience food, takeout containers, the occasional dinner at a colleague's home. The mechanics of meal preparation had become foreign.

Yet the sounds were familiar. Universal. Someone caring for someone else through food.
