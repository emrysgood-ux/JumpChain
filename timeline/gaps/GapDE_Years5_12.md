# PART 2: YEAR 5 (1993) — TRAINING BEGINS

## 2.1 — Context

The Year 4 revelation is still fresh. Sheldon and Yosho are establishing their new dynamic — equals with secrets, partners in training.

## 2.2 — Training Structure Established

**Daily practice (morning):**
- 5:30 AM: Wake
- 5:45-6:15 AM: Meditation together
- 6:15-6:30 AM: Control exercises
- 6:30 AM: Breakfast (Sheldon cooks)

**Weekly deep session (Sundays):**
- 2-hour extended practice
- More advanced techniques
- Discussion of progress/challenges

## 2.3 — What Sheldon Learns This Year

| Skill | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Sensory regulation basics | Can consciously dim enhanced senses |
| Energy awareness introduction | Begins sensing life force in living things |
| Grounding techniques | Anchoring during stress |

## 2.4 — Key Moment: Tenchi Asks About Grandfather

**Scene seed:**

Tenchi (8): "Sheldon-san, why doesn't Grandfather get old?"

Sheldon freezes mid-motion. Yosho, across the room, goes still.

Tenchi: "The kids at school say their grandparents look older every year. But Grandfather looks the same as when I was little."

**Sheldon's response:**
- Does not lie
- Does not reveal truth
- Redirects with partial truth

> "Some people age differently than others, Tenchi. Your grandfather has lived a very healthy life. The mountain air, the shrine work — it keeps him strong."

Tenchi accepts this. For now.

**Aftermath:** Sheldon and Yosho discuss how to handle future questions. They agree: let Tenchi discover in his own time. Don't burden a child with cosmic secrets.

## 2.5 — Household Status

- Nobuyuki visits most weekends
- Tenchi thriving in school
- Sheldon's repair business steady
- Village fully accepts the household

# PART 3: YEAR 6 (1994) — THE CHOICE

## 3.1 — Context

Tenchi is 9-10. Old enough to have opinions. Nobuyuki makes an offer.

## 3.2 — Key Event: Nobuyuki's Offer

**Background:** Nobuyuki's work in Kurashiki is stable. He has an apartment. He could take Tenchi to live with him full-time — better schools, more opportunities, city life.

**The offer:**
> "Tenchi, you could come live with me. There's a good school nearby. You'd have your own room. We could be together every day, not just weekends."

**Tenchi's response:**

He thinks about it. Seriously. For a week.

Then:
> "Dad, I love you. But this is my home. Grandfather needs me. Sheldon-san needs me. The shrine needs me."
>
> "I want to stay."

**Nobuyuki's reaction:** Sad, but understanding. He knows the shrine is good for his son. He doesn't push.

**Sheldon's internal response:** Relief mixed with responsibility. The boy chose to stay. Now Sheldon has to be worthy of that choice.

## 3.3 — Aftermath

This choice solidifies the household structure. Tenchi isn't here by default — he's here by decision. This will matter when canon events offer him other options.

## 3.4 — Training Progress

| New skill | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Energy sensing (refined) | Can identify individuals by their "presence" |
| Emotional grounding (advanced) | Stable during stress |
| First control of enhanced reflexes | Can slow himself to "normal" |

# PART 4: YEAR 7 (1995) — ASKING ABOUT MOTHER

## 4.1 — Context

Tenchi is 10-11. Elementary 5th grade. Old enough to ask harder questions.

## 4.2 — Historical Context

**1995 in Japan:**
- Great Hanshin Earthquake (January 17) — 6,400+ deaths
- Aum Shinrikyo sarin attack (March 20) — Tokyo subway
- National trauma year

The national grief may trigger Tenchi's personal grief.

## 4.3 — Key Event: Tenchi Asks About His Mother

**Setting:** Evening, after news coverage of earthquake memorial.

**The question:**
> "What was Mom like? Really like? Not just the stories."

**Who answers:**

Nobuyuki is present (weekend visit). He struggles — too close to the grief.

Yosho speaks — formal, respectful, surface-level.

Then Tenchi turns to Sheldon.

**The problem:** Sheldon never met Kiyone. She died before he arrived.

**Sheldon's response:**
> "I never knew her, Tenchi. I wish I had. But I know what she left behind."
>
> "I know she chose to live here, at the shrine, instead of somewhere easier. I know she raised you for four years with more love than some people give in a lifetime. I know your grandfather's eyes change when he talks about her. I know your father still can't say her name without his voice catching."
>
> "I know she's in you. The way you laugh. The way you care about people. The stubbornness when you think you're right."
>
> "I didn't know her. But I know her son. And I think... I think she'd be proud of who you're becoming."

**Tenchi's reaction:** Tears. But good tears. The kind that heal.

## 4.4 — Aftermath

This conversation deepens Sheldon's role as co-parent. He's not replacing Kiyone — he's honoring her by raising her son well.

# PART 5: YEAR 8 (1996) — PRE-ADOLESCENCE

## 5.1 — Context

Tenchi is 11-12. Final year of elementary school. Pre-adolescence is hitting.

## 5.2 — Changes in Tenchi

- More privacy-conscious
- Occasional mood swings
- Questions authority (gently)
- Developing his own interests
- Less physically affectionate

## 5.3 — Sheldon's Adaptation

He gives Tenchi space without withdrawing:
- Knocks before entering
- Asks instead of tells
- Respects "I don't want to talk about it"
- Stays available without hovering

## 5.4 — Training Progress

| New skill | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Presence suppression | Can dim his "signature" to appear normal |
| Physical control (advanced) | Enhanced abilities fully masked |
| Energy awareness (broad) | Can sense the tree, the cave, the land |

Sheldon notices: the tree feels alive. The cave feels... contained. He doesn't ask. Yosho doesn't tell.

## 5.5 — Household Milestone

**Sheldon's workshop expansion:** He builds a proper workshop/lab behind the shrine. Ostensibly for repairs. Actually for experiments. Yosho knows. No one else does.

# PART 6: YEAR 9 (1997) — MIDDLE SCHOOL

## 6.1 — The Transition

Middle school in Japan is a significant shift:
- New school (chūgakkō)
- Longer commute (bus to town)
- More demanding academics
- Club activities expected
- Uniforms, stricter rules

## 6.2 — Tenchi's Adjustment

He handles it well — he's resilient. But:
- Less time at home
- More tired
- New social dynamics
- First real exposure to "normal" families

## 6.3 — The "Growing Apart" Phase

**This is normal.** Adolescents separate from parental figures. Tenchi:
- Spends more time in his room
- Has friends Sheldon never meets
- Develops private interests
- Talks less about his day

**Sheldon's response:** He doesn't panic. He remembers his own adolescence. He stays consistent — available, non-intrusive, present.

## 6.4 — Training Continues

Despite the distance, morning meditation persists. It becomes the one touchpoint that doesn't change.

# PART 7: YEAR 10 (1998) — QUESTIONS ABOUT THE PAST

## 7.1 — Context

Tenchi is 13-14. Full adolescence. Old enough for complex questions.

## 7.2 — Key Event: Tenchi's Research

Tenchi has been looking through old family records. He finds:
- Photos that don't match the timeline
- References to relatives who should be dead
- Inconsistencies in the family history

**His question to Yosho:**
> "Grandfather... how old are you, really?"

**Yosho's response:** He doesn't lie. But he doesn't tell the full truth.
> "Old enough to know that some questions have answers you're not ready to hear."

**Tenchi:** "That's not an answer."

**Yosho:** "It's the only one I can give you right now. When you're ready, you'll understand."

**Tenchi's reaction:** Frustrated. But he trusts his grandfather. He shelves the question.

## 7.3 — Sheldon's Role

He supports both parties:
- Validates Tenchi's curiosity ("Your questions are good questions")
- Supports Yosho's boundaries ("He'll tell you when he can")
- Doesn't reveal what he knows

This is the first time Sheldon actively keeps a secret FROM Tenchi. It feels wrong. But it's right.

# PART 8: YEAR 11 (1999) — CALM BEFORE STORM

## 8.1 — Context

Tenchi is 14-15. Middle school 3rd year. High school entrance exams loom.

## 8.2 — Academic Focus

This year is consumed by entrance exams. Tenchi studies. Sheldon helps (math, science). The household runs quiet.

## 8.3 — The Sense of Something Coming

Both Yosho and Sheldon feel it:
- The cave pulses occasionally
- The tree seems restless
- Dreams are strange
- Energy fluctuations at night

**Conversation:**
> Yosho: "Something is shifting."
> Sheldon: "I feel it too."
> Yosho: "It may be soon."
> Sheldon: "What do we do?"
> Yosho: "We prepare. And we wait."

They don't know exactly what's coming. But 700 years of experience (Yosho) and trained awareness (Sheldon) both register: change approaches.

## 8.4 — Training Milestone

Sheldon's control is now complete. His abilities are fully integrated — enhanced but masked. He could pass any examination as "normal."

Yosho: "There is nothing more I can teach you about control. What comes next... you will have to discover yourself."

# PART 9: YEAR 12 (2000) — FINAL NORMAL YEAR

## 9.1 — Context

Tenchi is 15-16. High school 1st year. The last year before canon.

## 9.2 — High School Life

- Tenchi attends local high school
- Makes friends
- Joins kendo club (grandfather's influence)
- Normal teenager with normal life

## 9.3 — The Household at Peace

This is the peak of the "quiet years":
- Routines perfected over a decade
- Relationships deep and comfortable
- No crises, no drama
- A family in full function

## 9.4 — Sheldon's Internal State

He's been here 12 years. This is his life now. Not a temporary situation — home.

> *I came here expecting to figure out what I am, who sent me, what I'm supposed to do.*
>
> *Instead, I found a family. A son. A... whatever Yosho is to me.*
>
> *Maybe that's the answer. Maybe the purpose is just... this.*

## 9.5 — Yosho's Observation

Yosho has watched Sheldon for 12 years. He's noted:
- Sheldon doesn't age
- His power remains stable
- He could leave at any time but chooses to stay
- He loves Tenchi as a son

For the first time in centuries, Yosho considers: this person might stay. Really stay.

## 9.6 — The Tension Building

Throughout Year 12:
- The cave's energy pulses increase
- The tree sends warning sensations
- Tenchi has strange dreams he doesn't report
- Yosho grows increasingly watchful

By spring of Year 13 (April 2001), everyone in the household feels it:

Something is about to happen.
