# TENTH CENTURY (2889-2989): COMPLETION

## Years 900-1000

### The Final Century

| Period | Events |
|--------|--------|
| 2889-2950 | Continuing life; still living, loving, growing |
| 2950-2980 | Slowing; Yosho withdraws from public responsibilities |
| 2980-2988 | Final years; deep intimacy, preparation |
| April 2989 | **THE PASSING** |

### Yosho's Final Years

- Prefers shrine, garden, Sheldon's company
- Long periods of meditation with Funaho (his tree)
- "I am saying goodbye to my tree. She understands."
- Not a death watch—still living fully

### THE PASSING (April 2989)

**Setting:** Spring morning. Cherry blossoms falling—as when Sheldon arrived 1,000 years before.

**The Moment:** Yosho does not wake. Face peaceful. Hand rests in Sheldon's. No visible aging—looks as he did when they met. But something within has completed.

**Statistics:**
- 1,700 years of existence
- 1,000 with Sheldon
- Finally, rest

### THE MOURNING

| Event | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| Funeral | Spans one week; representatives from every major civilization |
| Jurai | Emperor attends personally |
| Private moments | Tenchi weeping; Ryoko crying (who never cried); Ayeka embracing brother's memory |
| Burial | Yosho interred beside Funaho; ship and captain together in earth |

### THE WEEK AFTER

Sheldon sits by grave. Cosmos asks:

> "You did well, my son. You loved well. Are you ready?"

> "One week. Let me say goodbye properly."

### THE DEPARTURES (Final Gifts)

| Recipient | Gift |
|-----------|------|
| Tenchi | Letter for every descendant yet to be born |
| Ryoko | Weapon worthy of legend |
| Ayeka | Political treatise on leading through love |
| Washu | Research notes spanning 1,000 years |
| Sasami/Tsunami | Message for Cosmos if they should ever meet |
| Village | Endowment lasting another millennium |
| Shrine | Every protection woven into stones |

### THE FINAL NIGHT

Sheldon at Yosho's grave. Cherry blossoms fall.

> "See you on the other side of forever."

### THE DEPARTURE (July 2989)

**Three months after Yosho's death:**
- Sheldon grieves, says goodbye to descendants
- Cosmos asks: "Are you ready?"
- Final dawn: Sheldon walks into light
- Jump transition activates
- Universe One: Complete

> "Completion. Not an ending—a fulfillment."
