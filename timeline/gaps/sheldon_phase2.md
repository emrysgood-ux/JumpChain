## THE REVELATION YEAR (Year 4)

### Trigger
Sheldon demonstrates an ability he cannot explain (perfect night vision). Katsuhito notices. Sheldon realizes he cannot keep lying to the man who trusts him.

### The Conversation
Sheldon tells Yosho:
- He died in another life
- He woke up in this body with no memory of how
- Someone (he doesn't know who) gave him this chance
- He has abilities he doesn't understand

### Yosho's Response
- Listens completely
- Believes without question
- Does NOT reveal his own nature
- Offers to help Sheldon understand his abilities
- Offers formal training

### Aftermath
- Equals with secrets
- True partnership begins
- Training becomes more serious
- Yosho watches more carefully
