### The Village Speech

The village elders who remember Sheldon's arrival 25 years ago finally address the obvious:

> "The American hasn't aged. The priest hasn't aged. Strange women live at the shrine. Ships appear in the sky."

Their conclusion:

> "We don't understand. We don't need to. The shrine protects this village. That's enough."

**Significance:** Community acceptance without explanation. Trust earned over decades.
