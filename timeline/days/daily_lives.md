## STORY INTEGRATION NOTES

### Establishing Routine = Establishing Trust

The daily patterns serve narrative functions:
1. **Predictability creates safety** — Tenchi thrives on routine
2. **Shared labor builds bonds** — Working together creates family
3. **Rhythm shows integration** — Sheldon becoming part of the pattern
4. **Deviation creates drama** — Breaking routine signals change

### Key Routine Moments for Character Development

**Sheldon:**
- First time allowed to wake Tenchi alone
- First time sent to village alone
- First time Katsuhito asks for his cooking
- First time called "family" by action not word

**Tenchi:**
- First time reaches for Sheldon's hand naturally
- First time asks Sheldon to read bedtime story
- First time introduces Sheldon to friend as family

**Katsuhito:**
- First time leaves shrine in Sheldon's care
- First time shares evening tea as equals
- First time speaks of personal matters
