## Overview

**Date:** Monday, April 3, 1989
**Weather:** Clear spring day, warming afternoon
**Location:** Asakuchi District → Masaki Shrine, Okayama Prefecture, Japan
**POV:** Sheldon Tsukino (close third)

**Summary:** Sheldon awakens in a rice field with no memory of how he arrived, discovers his identity documents, and finds his way to Masaki Shrine where he is offered a place to stay.

## Hour-by-Hour Timeline

### 2:47 PM — The Awakening
**Scene 1: The Awakening**
- Sheldon wakes face-down in agricultural field
- No memory of arrival; last memory is Alaska (death)
- Body feels young, healthy, unfamiliar
- First warmth in chest sensation (unidentified)
- Observes environment: rice paddies, distant mountains, spring flowers
- Uguisu (Japanese bush warbler) call — first sound of new life
- Begins walking toward road

**Sensory Details:**
- Smell: Fresh earth, green growth
- Sound: Uguisu, wind in rice stalks
- Touch: Soil on face, sun on back
- Sight: Sumire (violets) at field edge

### 2:52 PM — The Road
**Scene 2: The Road**
- Reaches rural asphalt road
- Discovers wallet in back pocket
- Contents: ¥500,000 cash, Residence Card, photograph
- Learns name "Sheldon Tsukino" from card
- Recognizes "Tsukino" as Sailor Moon surname (dismisses as coincidence)
- Photograph shows elderly Japanese woman (unknown)
- Registers birthdate: August 21, 1963 (age 25)
- Registered address in Okayama Prefecture

### 3:00-3:30 PM — Walking
- Walks west on rural road
- Processing confusion, testing body
- Notes physical capability exceeds expectation
- Warmth in chest recurs (still unidentified)

### ~3:30 PM — The Farmer
**Scene 3: The Farmer**
- Yamamoto Takeshi stops his truck, offers ride
- First human interaction in new life
- Sheldon learns about area: near Kurashiki
- Yamamoto mentions "Tsukino farm" — old family, passed away
- Notices omamori charm from Masaki Shrine in truck
- Learns shrine is nearby, has elderly priest
- Dropped at bus stop for shrine area

### ~4:15 PM — The Bus
**Scene 4: The Bus**
- Takes local bus toward shrine
- Driver: Nakamura Kenji (taciturn, helpful)
- Observes rural Okayama through window
- Disembarks at Jinja-mae stop

### ~4:45 PM — The Steps
**Scene 5: The Steps**
- Arrives at base of Masaki Shrine steps
- Counts: 320 steps to top
- Climbs without significant fatigue (notes strangeness)
- Body performs better than expected
- SEED-004 reinforced: dormant abilities hint

### ~5:00 PM — First Meeting: Tenchi
**Scene 6: First Meeting — Tenchi**
- Reaches shrine grounds
- Five-year-old boy playing alone: Tenchi Masaki
- Tenchi's immediate trust, no fear of stranger
- First "Sheldon-nii" usage (spontaneous)
- Sheldon experiences first found-family pull
- Tenchi leads him toward main building

### ~5:15 PM — First Meeting: Katsuhito
**Scene 7: First Meeting — Katsuhito**
- Meets Katsuhito Masaki in shrine building
- Katsuhito's impossible grace first noted (SEED-001)
- Eyes that seem older than face suggests
- Brief explanation: lost, seeking direction
- Katsuhito offers arrangement: work for lodging
- "The shrine has room for those who work."
- Formal acceptance, conditional

### ~5:30-6:00 PM — First Meeting: Rea
**Scene 9: First Meeting — Rea** *(NEEDS WRITING)*
- Rea returns from high school (new semester activities)
- Finds stranger in residence
- Protective wariness toward Tenchi
- Assessment of Sheldon's trustworthiness
- Quiet introduction, minimal information shared
- Household dynamic established: Katsuhito (wisdom), Rea (practical care), Sheldon (newcomer)

### ~6:30 PM — The Tour and Warning
**Scene 8: Evening — The Cave Warning**
- Katsuhito shows Sheldon the shrine grounds
- Points out: main building, residence, Funaho tree, gardens
- **The Forbidden Cave:** "You are not to approach that cave."
- No explanation given
- SEED-002 planted: what's in the cave?
- Environmental unease near cave entrance

### ~7:00-9:00 PM — Evening
- Simple dinner (Rea's preparation)
- Shown to small room that will become his
- Futon, basic furniture, clean
- Lies awake processing impossible day
- Warmth in chest as comfort
- Falls asleep in new life
