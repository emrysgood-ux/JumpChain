## PART I: LIGHT HAWK WINGS — OFFENSIVE FUNCTIONS

### 1.1 Wing Count Progression

| Year Range | LHW Capability | Combat Status |
|------------|----------------|---------------|
| 1-12 | 0 (dormant) | Cannot manifest |
| 13-20 | 1 (unstable) | Emergency only |
| 21-30 | 3 (stable) | Reliable offensive use, comparable to 1st Gen treeships |
| 31-50 | 5 (developed) | Significant offensive power |
| 51-100 | 7 (advanced) | Major combat capability |
| 100+ | 10 (maximum normal) | Peak 3D power, planetary-scale attacks |
| 500+ | 13 (transcendent) | Ultimate potential, exceeds local physics |

### 1.2 Why 13 Exceeds Normal Maximum

- **10 LHW** = Maximum possible in 3D space (local physics limit)
- **13 LHW** = Trans-dimensional influence from Cosmos (operates outside Tenchi universe's dimensional structure)
- **Wings 11-13** carry silver-white Cosmos signature
- Represents external origin, not Juraian/Chousin power

### 1.3 Beam Mode (OFFENSIVE)

| Aspect | Specification |
|--------|---------------|
| **Function** | Wings focus into energy projector |
| **Output** | Extremely destructive — planetary-scale damage potential |
| **Defense Penetration** | Cannot be blocked except by equal shields or planetary masses |
| **Charge Time** | Several hours for planetary bombardment; seconds for anti-ship fire |
| **Limitation** | Cannot attack AND defend simultaneously |

**Planetary Bombardment Specifics:**
- A single 3-wing entity CAN glass a planet
- Takes **weeks** for one entity to complete
- Requires **several hours** to charge
- Uses **minimum 3 wings** for the bombardment beam
- Leaves **zero wings for defense** during charge/fire

### 1.4 Matter Conversion (OFFENSIVE)

| Construct | Combat Application |
|-----------|-------------------|
| **Blade** | Edge measured in **Planck lengths** — absolute sharpness |
| **Armor** | Protective covering with offensive implications |
| **Other Tools** | Various combat applications as needed |

**Critical Blade Property:**
> Light Hawk constructs **never cut anything you don't want them to cut**

This is a SAFETY feature, not a limitation. The blade CAN cut anything; it simply won't do so accidentally.

## PART II: JUMPCHAIN PERK — WINGS OF THE LIGHTHAWK

### 2.1 Perk Specifications

**Source:** Ultimatum Perk (600CP)
**Unlock:** After completing both Slice of Life and Space Opera modes

**Capabilities:**
- Manifest Light Hawk Wings WITHOUT Royal Tree
- Initially limited to ONE wing
- Train to THREE wings by end of ten years
- Single wing defends against infinite minor attacks
- Wings convert to matter: armor or bladed weapons with **Planck-length edges**

### 2.2 Critical Distinction

This perk doesn't grant an alien ability — Sheldon's Cosmos Seed already contains the potential. The Tenchi Muyo universe's cosmic laws provide the **expression pathway** for Lambda energy to manifest AS Light Hawk Wings.

## PART VI: CORRECTED DOCUMENTATION

### 6.1 Philosophy vs. Capability

**CAPABILITY (what he CAN do):**
- Generate up to 13 Light Hawk Wings
- Fire planetary-bombardment beams
- Create Planck-edge weapons
- Inherit Senshi combat abilities
- Build ray guns and combat technology
- Weaponize Lambda Power

**PHILOSOPHY (what he CHOOSES to do):**
- First instinct: Shield others
- Offensive use: Reserved for genuine threats only
- Never uses LHW for intimidation or display
- Prefers protective/support role

### 6.2 The Distinction

Some documentation stated "cannot generate offensive power" or "non-combatant status" — these referred to Sheldon's **ROLE AND PHILOSOPHY**, not his **CAPABILITIES**.

**Sheldon is the son of a warrior goddess.**
**Sailor Cosmos fought Chaos itself.**
**"Senshi" means soldier.**

The inheritance is real. The capability exists. The choice to use it defensively is just that — a choice.
