## YEAR 2 (April 1990 - March 1991)
**Tenchi: Age 5→6 | School: Kindergarten 年長 (nenchō - final year) | Power Phase: I**

- Tenchi in final kindergarten year
- Sheldon fully integrated into village life
- Relationship with Katsuhito: Wary respect evolving
- Historical context: Bubble economy at peak, about to burst
- Key theme: Stability and routine

---

## YEAR 3 (April 1991 - March 1992)
**Tenchi: Age 6→7 | School: Elementary 1st Grade | Power Phase: I**

- Tenchi begins elementary school (Shougakkou)
- New schedule adjustments for everyone
- Bubble economy collapsing; village somewhat insulated
- Sheldon's role evolving: Helper → trusted presence
- Gulf War (Jan 1991) - distant world events

---

## YEAR 4: THE REVELATION YEAR (April 1992 - March 1993)
**Tenchi: Age 7→8 | School: Elementary 2nd Grade | Power Phase: II begins**

### Critical Events:
- **July 1992**: Tenchi begins sword training with Katsuhito ("First Blade")
- **[Date TBD]**: Sheldon tells Yosho the truth about his reincarnation
- Yosho's response: Initial skepticism → gradual acceptance
- Relationship shifts to "true peer" acknowledgment
- Power Phase II: Subtle emergence begins (environmental effects)

### Significance:
This is the pivotal year where Sheldon transitions from "mysterious helper" to someone Yosho can genuinely trust with knowledge of the larger situation.

---

## YEAR 5 (April 1993 - March 1994)
**Tenchi: Age 8→9 | School: Elementary 3rd Grade | Power Phase: II**

- Post-revelation: Sheldon-Yosho relationship deepening
- Tenchi's training continues; natural talent emerging
- Joint training sessions begin (Sheldon + Yosho + Tenchi)
- Theme: Trust solidifying

---

## YEAR 6 (April 1994 - March 1995)
**Tenchi: Age 9→10 | School: Elementary 4th Grade | Power Phase: II**

- Training intensifying
- Tenchi entering pre-adolescence
- Historical: Great Hanshin Earthquake (Jan 17, 1995) - distant but impactful news
- Theme: Growth and preparation

---

## YEAR 7 (April 1995 - March 1996)
**Tenchi: Age 10→11 | School: Elementary 5th Grade | Power Phase: II**

- Final years of pure childhood
- Sword skills developing noticeably
- Sheldon's role: Uncle/mentor figure cemented
- Theme: Last innocent years

---

## YEAR 8 (April 1996 - March 1997)
**Tenchi: Age 11→12 | School: Elementary 6th Grade (final year) | Power Phase: II**

- Preparing for middle school transition
- Elementary school graduation (March 1997)
- Theme: Transition and endings

---

## YEAR 9 (April 1997 - March 1998)
**Tenchi: Age 12→13 | School: Middle School 1st Year (Chuugakkou) | Power Phase: II**

- Middle school begins
- Adolescence in full swing
- Normal independence phase begins
- Asian financial crisis (1997) - economic ripples
- Theme: Growing up

---

## YEAR 10 (April 1998 - March 1999)
**Tenchi: Age 13→14 | School: Middle School 2nd Year | Power Phase: II**

- Teenage years; typical adolescent development
- Training continues but adjusted for school demands
- Seal awareness: Yosho monitoring more closely
- Theme: Adolescent normalcy

---

## YEAR 11 (April 1999 - March 2000)
**Tenchi: Age 14→15 | School: Middle School 3rd Year (final) | Power Phase: II**

- Final middle school year
- High school entrance exams
- Y2K preparations (Dec 1999-Jan 2000)
- Seal weakening becoming more apparent
- Theme: Anticipation

---

## YEAR 12 (April 2000 - March 2001)
**Tenchi: Age 15→16 | School: High School 1st Year (Koukou) | Power Phase: III (Mature)**

- High school begins
- **Final "normal" year before canon**
- Seal approaching critical weakness
- Sheldon and Yosho: Final preparations
- Power Phase III: Sheldon's abilities mature
- Theme: Calm before the storm

---

## YEAR 13: CANON BEGINS (April 2001+)
**Tenchi: Age 16→17 | School: High School 2nd Year | Power Phase: IV (Canon Events)**

### Canon Trigger:
- Ryoko awakens when Tenchi (age 17) enters the cave and removes the sword
- This sets in motion all OVA Episode 1 events
- Sheldon's 12+ years of preparation face their test

### Key Canon Events (OVA Timeline):
1. Ryoko released from 700-year imprisonment
2. Ayeka arrives pursuing Ryoko
3. Sasami accompanies Ayeka
4. Mihoshi crashes
5. Washu released from subspace
6. [Additional canon events per OVA episodes]
