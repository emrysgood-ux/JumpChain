The ache in his muscles was a kind of proof. Sheldon lay in the pre-dawn darkness, cataloging each complaint: the pull across his shoulders, the tightness in his forearms, the general protest of a body that had worked for the first time in its brief existence.

## SCENE 252 | 05:22 | Guest Room - Masaki Shrine
**Type:** Character Development
**Location:** Guest room, pre-dawn

His former body had never ached like this. Desk work produced different complaints—lower back, neck, the strain of eyes focused too long on equations. Those were sedentary pains, the cost of a life spent in chairs.
