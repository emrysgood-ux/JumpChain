## Background

Amane comes from a wealthy, influential family in the galactic community. Before joining the Galaxy Police, she was:
- A successful model
- Heir to family wealth
- Living a privileged existence

### Why She Joined GP
Despite her glamorous background, Amane wanted more:
- Sought meaning beyond modeling
- Desired to prove herself on her own merits
- Rebelled against family expectations
- Genuinely wanted to help people

## Relationship with Seina

Amane initially saw Seina as:
- A curiosity
- A recruit she was responsible for
- Someone pathetic but strangely endearing

This evolved into:
- Genuine attraction (despite herself)
- Protective feelings
- Respect for his courage
- Love (eventually marriage)

### The Rivalry
Amane competes with Kiriko and others for Seina's attention. Their rivalry is:
- Genuine but not hostile
- Often comedic
- Eventually resolved through polygamous marriage

---

## The Seto Connection

Seto has taken special interest in Amane:
- Sees her potential beyond modeling
- Uses her as a project in matchmaking schemes
- Arranged her assignment to Seina's unit
- Views Amane as "reformable" aristocrat

Amane is simultaneously grateful for and wary of Seto's attention.
