## CHAPTER SYNOPSIS

Sheldon arrives at Masaki Shrine and begins building connections. Awakening in a chrysanthemum field near Shiroato castle ruins with no memory of how he arrived but full knowledge of his death in Alaska, Sheldon discovers an identity waiting for him—Tsukino Sheldon, heir to his mother's family home. The chapter follows his first month integrating into the Masaki household while processing his impossible resurrection.

## KEY SCENES

### 1. The Awakening (Scenes 1-50)
Sheldon wakes face-down in grass, remembers dying in Alaska, discovers his body has been "fixed" rather than healed. Finds identity documents for a life he doesn't remember.

### 2. First Contact (Scenes 145-180)
Meets Masaki Katsuhito at shrine. The old priest expected him—"The mountain accepted you."

### 3. Meeting Tenchi (Scenes 180-220)
Four-year-old Tenchi approaches with serious curiosity. Foundation of their relationship established.

### 4. The Letter (Scenes 256-258)
Sheldon reads uncle Kiyoshi's letter. Discovers he has living family who expect him to return to them eventually.
