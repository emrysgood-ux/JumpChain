## PHASE 1: CANON CONCLUSION & STABILIZATION (Years 14-18)

### Year 14 (2002): Canon Events Wrapping Up

**OVA 2 Events:**
- Dr. Clay/Zero arc
- Tokimi's influence introduced
- Zero develops emotions, eventually merges with Ryoko
- Galaxy Police formally aware of Earth

**Sheldon's Role:**
- Emotional support for household
- Manages domestic logistics during crises
- Does NOT participate in combat
- Shares limited truth with Washu (Cosmos, JumpChain framework)

**Key Moment:** First explicit acknowledgment between Yosho and Sheldon
> "I don't know what I'd do without you."

### Year 15-16 (2003-2004): Domestic Rhythm

**Household Status:**
- 10+ permanent residents
- Aunt dynamic fully solidified
- Tenchi in university
- Sheldon-Yosho at "life partners in all but name" stage

**Daily Life:**
- Cooking rotations established
- Chore distributions negotiated
- Space for each personality type
- Conflicts resolved through family meetings

### Year 17 (2005): Z Crisis

**OVA 3 Events:**
- Z attacks
- Chousin revelation
- Tenchi's divine nature confirmed (Kami Tenchi emerges, chooses mortality)
- Noike arrives; integrates as family, NOT arranged bride

**Sheldon's First LHW Flicker:**
- Crisis moment during Z attack
- Unconscious manifestation
- Single wing, unstable, immediately fades
- Washu: "Fascinating. You shouldn't be able to do that."

### Year 18 (2006): Canon Complete

**Status at End of Canon:**
| Character | Role |
|-----------|------|
| Tenchi | Young adult; cosmic entity; shrine heir |
| Yosho | Elder; Sheldon's partner; true form known to family |
| Sheldon | Co-patriarch; first LHW manifestation witnessed |
| Ryoko | Integrated; "chaotic aunt"; merged with Zero |
| Ayeka | Princess; aunt figure; political liaison |
| Sasami | Growing; Tsunami integration continuing |
| Mihoshi | GP officer; beloved chaos agent |
| Washu | Scientist; Chousin; fascinated by Sheldon |
| Noike | Newest member; family role |
| Ryo-Ohki | Cabbit; treeship; beloved pet |

## PHASE 2: RECOVERY & EXPANSION (Years 19-25)

**Note:** This is NOT his full potential (13 LHW). This is his working capacity during this era.

### Year 19-20 (2007-2008): Recovery Focus

**Priorities:**
- Domestic peace after years of canon chaos
- Tenchi's university completion
- Yosho-Sheldon partnership deepening
- First children of extended family (Tenchi's generation) being discussed

**Sheldon's Work:**
- Independent physics research
- Scientific patents filed
- Conference attendance (maintains academic credibility)
- Occasional consultation work

**Key Development:** Hair braiding, late-night tea, unspoken devotion between Yosho and Sheldon reaches "everyone sees it but them" stage.

### Year 21 (2009): First Interstellar Mission

**Tenchi's Leadership Development:**
- Diplomatic mission with Ayeka to minor Jurai colony
- First time representing Earth/Masaki interests off-world
- Sheldon stays home (anchor role)
- Mission successful; Tenchi proves capable

**Sheldon's Cosmic Artisan Abilities Mature:**
- Crafting cosmic-tier tools
- Interfacing with Juraian ships
- Technical reputation growing

### Year 22 (2010): Jurai Formal Recognition

**The Spatial Collapse Incident:**
- Juraian diplomatic vessel visits shrine
- Unexpected dimensional tear appears
- Sheldon unconsciously stabilizes it
- Jurai witnesses his power firsthand

**Political Consequence:**
- Jurai realizes Sheldon "isn't mortal"
- Formal recognition of his status begins
- Diplomatic classification: "Cosmic Entity, Allied"

### Year 23 (2011): GP Classification

**Galaxy Police Action:**
- Earth formally classified as "Protected World"
- Shrine designated "Culturally Significant Site"
- Regular GP patrols in solar system
- Kiyone Makibi assigned as liaison (different from Tenchi's mother)

### Year 24 (2012): Full Recovery

**Sheldon's Status:**
- 5 Light Hawk Wings stable at will
- Cosmic senses fully operational
- Recognized across multiple civilizations
- Still chooses domestic life over cosmic adventure

**Relationship Milestone:**
- Yosho proposes via Juraian ship-bond tradition
- Engagement period begins
- Wedding planned for ~Year 36 (2025)

### Year 25 (2014): The Village Speech

**The "We Know" Moment:**

Village elders who remember Sheldon's arrival 25 years ago finally address the obvious:
- "The American hasn't aged."
- "The priest hasn't aged."
- "Strange women live at the shrine."
- "Ships appear in the sky."

**Their Conclusion:**
> "We don't understand. We don't need to. The shrine protects this village. That's enough."

**Significance:**
- Village formally acknowledges supernatural elements
- Protection becomes mutual and explicit
- Community integration complete

## ADVENTURE TYPES (Years 19-25)

### Category 1: Diplomatic Missions
- Juraian colony visits
- Trade negotiations
- Cultural exchanges
- Mediation requests

### Category 2: Scientific Expeditions
- Washu's research projects (Sheldon assists)
- Archaeological discoveries
- Technology assessments
- Academic conferences (off-world)

### Category 3: Family Business
- Rescuing wayward relatives
- Attending Juraian court functions
- Dealing with political suitors for household members
- Managing property across systems

### Category 4: Crisis Response
- Dimensional instabilities
- Pirate incursions near Earth
- Rogue elements targeting Tenchi
- Natural disasters requiring cosmic intervention

### Category 5: Slice-of-Life (Off-World)
- Galactic festivals
- Shopping trips to alien markets
- Visiting Sasami's cooking teachers
- Ryoko's old haunts (reformed tourism)

## KEY RELATIONSHIPS DEVELOPMENT

### Sheldon & Yosho
- Years 19-24: "Life partners in all but name"
- Year 24: Formal engagement
- Daily intimacy without labels gives way to acknowledged commitment

### Sheldon & Tenchi
- Tenchi increasingly sees Sheldon as co-parent figure
- Leadership handoff discussions begin
- Tenchi starts seeking Sheldon's counsel on cosmic matters

### Sheldon & Washu
- Scientific collaboration deepens
- Washu's fascination with his anomalous nature continues
- Mutual respect as peers (she didn't invent the Birthing Matrix; he did)

### Sheldon & Village
- 25 years of trust-building pays off
- No longer "the foreigner" but "our Tsukino-san"
- Integrated into community memory
