## KEY CANON FACTS

### Characters

**Tenchi Masaki**
- Born: ~1984
- Parents: Nobuyuki + Kiyone MASAKI
- Sister: Tennyo (~80 years older)
- Potential: 10+ Light Hawk Wings (transcendent)

**Yosho/Katsuhito**
- Age: ~700 years
- True form: Young, purple hair/eyes
- Power: Barriers through Funaho (NOT LHW)
- Marriages: Kasumi (deceased), Airi (living)

**Ryoko**
- Created by Washu from Masu + ovum
- Age: ~5,000 years
- Controlled by Kagato most of existence
- Three gems contain power portions

**Washu**
- One of three Chousin
- Goddess of wisdom
- Sealed memories, lives as "mortal"
- Created Ryoko, Ryo-Ohki

**Tsunami**
- One of three Chousin
- Goddess of life
- Assimilated with Sasami
- Tree is origin of all Royal Trees

**Tokimi**
- One of three Chousin
- Goddess of necessity
- Most detached from mortals
- Appears OVA 3

---

### Power Systems

**Light Hawk Wings**
- Defense + offense
- Generated by Trees or innately (Tenchi)
- Wing count indicates power level
- Yosho does NOT generate (uses barriers)

**Royal Trees**
- Living spaceships
- 1st Gen: Direct Tsunami descendant (3 wings)
- 2nd Gen: Can generate (3 wings, need override)
- 3rd Gen: Limited (1 wing, need pair)

**Chousin**
- Created 22-dimensional reality
- Running experiment to find "beyond"
- Tenchi may be the answer

---

### Locations

**Jurai**
- Largest galactic empire
- Shogunate-style politics
- Royal family tree-bonded
- Capital: Jurai planet

**Earth**
- Yosho's exile location
- Funaho rooted here
- "Undeveloped world"
- Protected by obscurity

**Galaxy Academy**
- Airi is headmaster
- Scientific/educational center
- Minaho works here
