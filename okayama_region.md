**Category:** Location Reference

## TRANSPORTATION HUB: OKAYAMA STATION

**Distance from Kamogata:** ~30 minutes by local train
**Significance:** Gateway to Shinkansen network, regional transit hub

**1989 Shinkansen Service:**
- To Tokyo: ~3.5 hours (Hikari)
- To Kyoto: ~1 hour
- To Hiroshima: ~40-60 minutes
- To Shin-Osaka: ~50 minutes

**Train Types:**
| Type | Speed | Stops |
|------|-------|-------|
| Nozomi | Fastest | Major stations only |
| Hikari | Fast | More stops |
| Kodama | Local | All stations |

## DAY TRIP DESTINATIONS

### KURASHIKI (15 minutes from Okayama)

**Character:** Historic merchant quarter, Edo-period atmosphere
**Known For:** 
- Kurashiki Bikan Historical Quarter (white-walled warehouses)
- Canal boat rides through historic district
- Ohara Museum of Art (Western art collection since 1930)
- Ivy Square (converted textile factory)

**Story Potential:**
- Family outings
- Art appreciation scenes
- Romantic atmosphere for future characters
- Cultural contrast with rural shrine life

**Local Specialty (Omiyage):** Kibidango (millet dumplings)

---

### HIROSHIMA (90 minutes by Shinkansen)

**Character:** Peace Memorial City, sobering history
**Known For:**
- Hiroshima Peace Memorial Park
- Atomic Bomb Dome (UNESCO World Heritage)
- Hiroshima Peace Memorial Museum
- Miyajima Island (floating torii gate, 30 min ferry)

**Story Potential — CRITICAL FOR SHELDON:**
- As an American, confronting atomic history
- Emotional weight of visiting Peace Museum
- Processing complicated feelings about nationality
- Miyajima as counterpoint (beauty after devastation)

**Local Specialty (Omiyage):** Momiji manjū (maple leaf cakes)

**⚠️ SCENE SEED:** Sheldon's first visit to Hiroshima could be a major emotional beat. His American perspective vs. Japanese collective memory. Internal conflict. NO easy resolution.

---

### HIMEJI (45 minutes by Shinkansen)

**Character:** Castle town, martial history
**Known For:**
- Himeji Castle (UNESCO World Heritage, "White Heron Castle")
- Largest surviving feudal castle in Japan
- Koko-en Garden (reconstructed Edo gardens)

**Story Potential:**
- Katsuhito may have historical connection (samurai heritage)
- Architectural appreciation
- Photography opportunities
- School trip destination

**Note:** Castle underwent major restoration 1956-1964, fully accessible in 1989

---

### ONOMICHI (30 minutes from Okayama)

**Character:** Hillside temple town, nostalgic atmosphere
**Known For:**
- Temple Walk (25 temples along hillside path)
- Cat Alley (cat-themed shops, strays)
- Film location (Ozu films)
- Literary connections (Fumiko Hayashi)

**Story Potential:**
- Quiet contemplative day trips
- Sheldon processing thoughts through walking
- Cats as recurring motif
- Less touristy alternative to major sites

---

### TOMO-NO-URA (90 minutes total travel)

**Character:** Preserved Edo port town
**Known For:**
- One of most well-preserved port towns in Japan
- Barely changed since Edo period
- Inspiration for Ponyo (Miyazaki) — but film not released until 2008
- Historic lighthouse, merchant houses

**Story Potential:**
- Time capsule atmosphere
- Historical continuity themes
- Fishing village life observation
- Photography subject

**⚠️ 1989 Note:** In 1989, this is a sleepy fishing town, not yet famous from Ponyo. Authentic discovery opportunity.

---

### MOUNT DAISEN (Tottori Prefecture)

**Character:** Sacred mountain, pilgrimage site
**Known For:**
- One of Japan's most important sacred mountains
- Daisen-ji Temple (founded 718)
- Excellent hiking trails
- Clear views of Sea of Japan

**Story Potential:**
- Katsuhito may know this mountain spiritually
- Physical challenge for Sheldon
- Shrine/temple comparison to Masaki Shrine
- Seasonal beauty (autumn leaves, winter snow)

**Travel:** ~2 hours from Okayama by car/bus

## SETO INLAND SEA

**The Seto-Ohashi Bridge:** Opened April 10, 1988 — NEWLY ACCESSIBLE in story timeframe!

**Significance:** First time Honshu-Shikoku connected by rail/road

### Accessible Islands:

**Naoshima:** 
- In 1989: Just a small island with little tourism
- ⚠️ NOT YET an art destination (Benesse House opens 1992)
- Authentic fishing village experience

**Shodoshima:**
- Olive cultivation (Mediterranean atmosphere)
- Soy sauce production
- Twenty-Four Eyes film location
- Angel Road (tidal sandbar)

**Story Potential:**
- Island hopping adventures
- Maritime atmosphere different from mountains
- New bridge as symbol of modernity reaching rural areas

---

## SHIKOKU (via Seto-Ohashi Bridge)

**Accessible since April 1988**

### Kotohira (Kompira-san)
- 1,368 stone steps to main shrine
- One of most popular pilgrimage sites
- Tests endurance

### Ritsurin Garden (Takamatsu)
- One of finest gardens in Japan
- Katsuhito might appreciate the craftsmanship

### Naruto Whirlpools
- Natural phenomenon at strait
- Boat tours available

## TRAVEL LOGISTICS (1989)

**From Kamogata Village:**
1. Walk/bike to Kamogata Station
2. Local train to Okayama Station (~30 min)
3. Transfer to Shinkansen or regional lines

**Typical Day Trip Budget:**
- Train fare: ¥2,000-5,000 depending on distance
- Meals: ¥1,000-2,000
- Admission fees: ¥300-1,000
- Omiyage: ¥500-2,000

**Total:** ¥4,000-10,000 per person per day trip
