- Clumsy
- Easily confused
- Falls asleep at inappropriate times
- Causes collateral damage

**Hidden Truth:**
- Unnaturally lucky (possibly reality-bending)
- Stumbles into correct solutions
- Subconsciously brilliant tactical mind
- Washu suspects something anomalous about her luck patterns
