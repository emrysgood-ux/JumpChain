## SEASONAL FOODS

### By Season (Scene Reference)

**Spring (March-May)**
- Sakura mochi
- Takenoko (bamboo shoots)
- Nanohana (canola flowers)
- Strawberries

**Summer (June-August)**
- Somen (cold noodles)
- Hiyashi chuka
- Kakigōri (shaved ice)
- Watermelon
- Unagi (eel, especially Doyo no Ushi)

**Autumn (September-November)**
- Sanma (pacific saury)
- Matsutake mushrooms
- Kuri (chestnuts)
- Persimmons
- New rice

**Winter (December-February)**
- Nabe (hot pot)
- Oden
- Mochi
- Mikan (mandarin oranges)
- Daikon
