## The Dual Nature

Noike Kamiki Jurai is unique: she contains the astral form of the female Kagato within her body.

### The Female Kagato

When Washu created Kagato from Naja Akara's DNA, the clone initially manifested as female. The male Kagato emerged later and subjugated his female half, sealing her astral form. After male Kagato's death at Tenchi's hands, the female half needed a host.

**How She Came to Noike:**
- Female Kagato's astral form was discovered by Seto
- Seto adopted an orphan girl (Noike) specifically to house this entity
- The integration was (mostly) successful
- Female Kagato sleeps within Noike, occasionally influencing her

## Origin Story

Noike was orphaned at a young age and adopted by Seto Kamiki Jurai. Unlike most of Seto's "projects," Noike was chosen for a specific purpose: to serve as a vessel for the female Kagato's astral form.

### Life Under Seto

- Raised in House Kamiki with all its privileges and pressures
- Trained extensively in combat and administration
- Developed fierce loyalty to Seto despite knowing she was "chosen" for ulterior purposes
- Became a capable Galaxy Police officer
- Bonded with the 3rd-generation Royal Tree Kyoko

## The Engagement to Tenchi

**CANON:** Seto arranged Noike's engagement to Tenchi as part of her endless matchmaking schemes.

### The Arrangement
- Ostensibly to strengthen Masaki-Kamiki ties
- Actually serves multiple purposes for Seto
- Noike initially approached it as duty
- Complicated by genuine feelings that developed

### Noike's Position
She was sent to Earth to:
1. Observe the household Seto has monitored for centuries
2. Integrate with Tenchi and the other women
3. Fulfill her role as Seto's eyes and ears
4. Possibly awaken something in the female Kagato
